  2. Wulf Community Admin

    I've moved your post to its own thread, as you posted in a thread that doesn't relate to Experimental, and was also nearly a year old. Generally it's frowned upon when you bump threads that old.

    To answer your question, expecting to keep a world going long-term is pretty much a non-existent thing right now with Rust Experimental. The save version for worlds is often changes, which requires worlds to be wiped. However, you can try loading the latest save by adding -load "server/yourserveridentity/save/0.sav" to your command-line or startup script. You've more than likely already lost your old world though.
