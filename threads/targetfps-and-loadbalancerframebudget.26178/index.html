  5. CaptainNebulous

    I have put a fairly detailed description of loadbalancerframebudget in Console Commands - Official Hurtworld Wiki

    I am not a developer but I have tested this setting thoroughly, with maximum players of 50. But also note I use a modded map and I have increased the number of AI. Your results may vary depending on host and hardware. Everything should load fine up to the maximum value I have put in there, assuming the server can handle it. I found that 100 was far too low to keep the cycle rate above my preferred 1.5 once there was a lot of structures and players. If this doesn't work well for you then by all means use default.

    The cycle rate is largely affected by # of players, the ping of those players, # of AI, and # of structures

    TargetFPS may or may not be needed depending on your hardware. I don't use it, but it can come handy on some servers.
