    Can Noclip be added to this menu? It's tedious to keep typing it in console.

    Not sure if this helps with what you're after but I've always used no clip on a binded key it's so simple, just type in console..
    bind key noclip (Replace key with one of your choice that isn't already being used)
    e.g. (bind L noclip)
    Also I use god mode toggle on binded keys as follows: (type in console)
    bind key god 1
    bind key god 0
    e.g (bind G god 1)/(bind H god 0) - this sets your G/H key to automatically type "god 1"/"god 0" in console, from in-game without having to open your console menu every time.
