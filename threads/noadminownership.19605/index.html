  1. k1lly0u

    k1lly0u submitted a new resource:

    NoAdminOwnership - Remove fractional ownership from items when picked up or dropped for admins/players with permission

    Stop admin/players with permission from obtaining a ownership stake in items when picked up

    Permission
    noadminownership.ignore - For users who do not have auth level but require this feature (non admin moderators?)

    Chat Command
    /ownership - Toggles debug to see who is in the owner list and what fraction ownership they hold (when a item is dropped or picked up by the user)

    Config
    Code:
    {
  "IgnoreAuth1": true, // Remove ownership from Auth1
  "IgnoreAuth2":...
