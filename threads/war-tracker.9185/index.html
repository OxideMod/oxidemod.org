    Scorpyon submitted a new resource:

    War Tracker - Make wars official! Get regular updates for all players in warring guilds on the server.

    With this plugin, players and admins alike will be able to track warring guilds on the server as well as maintain a measure of control of war and peace between them.
    When a player declares war on another player, their guilds are officially in a state of war for a set duration.

    War Reports
    The server will keep everyone up to speed with any wars currently under way with regular reports on who is warring with who, and how long that war has left.
