    " "Votes": 0" means that he has never voted, the plugin marks that the player has made 0 votes.
    "Claimed": "0" means that the player hasn't voted yet.
    "ExpDate" is the date that is updated when voted, and it serves to not to vote before a certain time, set by you of course.
    The messages in the screen means that you haven't yet voted, and they show you the links to vote.
    [DOUBLEPOST=1479485569][/DOUBLEPOST]The player must vote at the link and after can write command /vote to take the rewards. The vote is given automatically if you are entering the server after the vote.
