  11. Ionstorm

    It is too many entity on your server, very most likely.

    We had this problem recently.

    If you have adjusted the spawn rate, change it to lower, write the server.writecfg and then restart.

    You will find your server will take 15mins to launch because it's deleting too many entity that shouldn't exist or well more than max allowed.

    Just restarting your server will temporary fix it after its deleted but it'll soon happen again, so best to fix the problem!

    Let me know if that applies /works/helps.

    PS. Also noticed that only some players have this issue. Thats because it seems they are in a heavy recourse zone, say next to two rad towns, forest and ore spawns. Where as people who aren't don't get it for a while longer.
