    Event - Headquarters - Capture the headquarters

    Alpha version 0.0.1

    Description:
    Headquarters is an Event for Event Manager where the goal is to capture and hold headquarters (marked as flags) as a TEAM.
    Each team will respawn next to a headquarter and will have to capture a headquarter.
    Once team A captured the headquarters, they won't be able to respawn until the headquarter has been destroyed by team B.
