    Ryan submitted a new resource:

    PermRewards - Gives players a kit-like reward if they're in an oxide group

    This plugin gives players a kit-like reward if they're in an oxide group.

    This is mainly aimed at servers who don't use kits and also don't want to freak out their playerbase thinking they have Kits installed. This plugins original purpose was to provide steam rewards to players, obviously to add the group to the players for that functionality you'd need a plugin such as Steam Groups | Oxide
