    RD156 submitted a new resource:

    Banks - Bank with account, tax and Cash in your Pocket

    This plugin is a Bank Plugin, all player have a Account and Cash.
    All player can move money between Account and Cash.
    Player can give money for player.
    If you deposit or transfert or withdrawal you have tax.
    This tax is customizable.
    This name of money is customizable.

    List command Player:
    /money (for display your money)
    /money [name of player] (for display the player's money)
    /give [player] [number] (for give money cash on player)
    /transfert [player] [number] (for give money account on...
