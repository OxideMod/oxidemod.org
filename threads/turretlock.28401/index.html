    redBDGR submitted a new resource:

    TurretLock - Give players the ability to lock their turrets

    This plugin allows players to add codelocks to their auto turrets to stop people from accessing them & forcing them to be destroyed instead.

    Permissions:
    • turretlock.use - players with this permission can use features of this plugin
    Chat Command:
    • /lockturret - locks the turret you are looking at if you have a codelock in your inventory
