    Inventory Manager - Allows you to clear, copy, view, and update player inventories

    Chat Commands:
    • /inv clear <player> - Clear's target's inventory and gear
    • /inv copy <player> - Replaces your inventory with their inventory. **WILL GET RID OF YOUR ITEMS**

  5. OrbDamu

    For those who are slightly confused. If you are an admin on your server and want to use this, type /grant user "YourName" inventorymanager.admin

    This will give you all functionalities

    If you just want to give an individual the ability to do one or a few of the commands insert their name where "YourName" is and replace the inventorymanager.admin permission with one of the other permissions
