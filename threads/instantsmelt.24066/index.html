  19. drew_benton

    Harold485 said: ↑
    Is there anyway to make it so they auto combine and i dont have to move like 8 stacks into each other?
    Click to expand...
    Try replacing all the:
    Code:
    CS.MoveToContainer(container, pos, true);
    with
    Code:
    CS.MoveToContainer(container);
    Which basically means rather than placing the new item at the old item's position, it'll let Rust handle the auto-stacking for you, or at least it should.
