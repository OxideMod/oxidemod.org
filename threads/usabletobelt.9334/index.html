  1. Wulf Community Admin

    AnExiledGod submitted a new resource:

    Usable To Belt - Any usable item will be moved to your belt if there is space.

    This plugin will move any items you craft in the categories Weapon, Tool, Medical, Food, and Construction to your belt (hotbar, action bar, whichever you call it) if there is an open space. (Favoring the first spot) This is great if your in a firefight and your gun breaks. Just craft another one and run around like a chicken with your head cut off until it pops into your belt slot, then you can commence wrecking your enemies.

  6. AnExiledGod

    AnExiledGod updated Usable To Belt with a new update entry:

    Fixed Item Duping Issue

    I have fixed the item duping issue by adding in a check to see if the item is already in your main inventory and if it's stack able.

    The issue was if you had a stack of something like C4 in your main inventory, and you crafted more C4 it was added to the stack of C4, but a copy was also put into your belt, and the copy couldn't be used or deleted.

    Sorry it took me a while to get this done!
