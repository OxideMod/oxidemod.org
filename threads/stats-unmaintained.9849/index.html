    Vilsol submitted a new resource:

    Stats - Plugin that collects various statistics about the players

    A very simple, yet very useful plugin. Collects different statistics and displays them on the website.

    It is your decision whether you want to allow your players to see the statistics, or to keep it private between the Admins.

    Currently collected statistics:
    • Player Kills
    • Player Deaths
    • Animal Kills
    • Item Crafts
    • Fired Bullets
    • Building Placements
    • Building Destructions
    • Gathered Resources
    More are going to be added as they will be added to the game...
