    PierreA submitted a new resource:

    RaidOffDefender - Kill people doing raidof or/and raidon

    Announce and kill people attacking structure when owner is offline. If raid on protection is active, kill also people who attack structure from online players. Otherwise just announce that a player base is under attack.

    Admin command
    - /raidOnToggle => Toggle raid on protection
