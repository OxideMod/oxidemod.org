  6. belboz

    Here is the fix I did. Can give more detail if people need it. Working great until they get it fixed.

    If your server is running and you have the LustyMap installed and it is in the constant 10 second retry, unload it with oxide.unload LustyMap from your console or rcon console.

    I went to playrust.io and did the generate map feature. Put in my seed and size. Generated the map and then downloaded it.

    Now you could just take that image and put it in your oxide/data/LustyMap directory. But it will be off some. playrust.io adds a little key legend around the right and bottom. You want to crop that out. So in your favorite paint program, drag from top left of image to the bottom right that just clips in the grids, but leaves out the right and bottom columns with the 1->26 A->Z stuff. If you do it right it should be a 1000x1000 pixel image. If it is real close you should be fine (mine came out 999x999).

    Now resize the image to 1440x1440

    Upload that image to your server in the oxide/data/LustyMap folder.

    Note the name. I called mine map.png

    Then in your LustyMap.cfg just edit the CustomMap_Filename to be "map.png" (or whatever yours is named)

    And also change CustomMap_Use to true

    Load the plugin and you are good to go.

    If you have any issue you can do the resetmap from the console and see if it fixes it.

    Working great for me.
     
    belboz, Mar 17, 2017
