  20. Steenamaroo

    Hey man,

    This is confirmed many times over, although I'm not knocking you taking the time and effort to add your stats.
    Unfortunately "NOTIFICATION: This plugin is no longer being developed or supported".

    I'm hoping one of the other devs might take over at some point but there's been a LOT of work going on remaking and revamping many of the older text-command-based plugs so, understandably, those guys are very busy.

    If you're not aware, the increasing mem use issue is directly related to enabling Rust:IO friends in your config.
    If you disable it and use friends plugin (or maybe clans?), you can get the same result at the cost of a minor inconvenience for your players.
    I've been doing that for a few months with no complaints.
    People generally use friends plugin anyway for set home rights etc.
