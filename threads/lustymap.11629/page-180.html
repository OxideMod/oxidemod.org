  19. -={U.S.P}=- DOCTROS

    This is what I have noticed with my LustyMap:

    1. Even though I have "ForceMapZoom" False, It still is forcing it to zoom on player connect.
    2. When I unzoom the map via the "-" tab, It shows me the whole map but the player icons flash on and off and I sometimes do not see helicopter or supply plane.
    3. After a little while, after I have set the minimap to show the whole map, with my flickering icons, That is when players start to get kicked with the "RPC Error in CL_ReceiveFilePng"

    I found that when people are getting kicked with this error, I stop the server, then I go to the "Storage" folder and delete all files in it. Then restart the server and players can now log back in with no error. (Caution, deleting the folders in the "Storage" folder will also remove any images you have uploaded to signs/picture frames with "SignArtist").

    I am sending this as an FYI, to hopefully figure out what might be causing these issues. Thank you to the creators of this plugin and for the time and effort you put in trying to stay on top of all the updates!
     
    -={U.S.P}=- DOCTROS, Feb 4, 2017
