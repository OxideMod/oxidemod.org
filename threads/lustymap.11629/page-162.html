    Just got mine working literally 10 minutes ago. I deleted the config file, the plugin file, and the files (NOT the folders) in /oxide/data/LustyMap/ . I then downloaded the latest LustyMap and harbor image. I put the harbor image in /oxide/data/LustyMap/Icons. I put the plugin in the plugins folder. I then opened the config file and disabled complex map, enabled custom map, and between the quotes next to custom map name I put "map.jpg". I then saved that config and went to Beancan.io. I typed in my seed and size then generated the map. I right clicked on the image and opened the image in new tab. I right clicked on the map image in the new tab and clicked save and named it 'map'. I then put it in my oxide/data/LustyMap directory. After that, I went to my server in game and in the console I typed "oxide.reload LustyMap" then after 5 mins typed "resetmap" then after another 5 mins I typed "oxide.reload LustyMap". After that, all 40 players had the map appear in the upper-left corner completely flawlessly. If you do exactly this and still don't see it, make sure you move around in game to be sure the map is still showing after sitting AFK (it disables if you don't move for a few seconds). Also, Relog if necessary. For anyone desperate to get a map up (like I was), do this. Any issues, just let me know and I'll do my best to help. Likes are appreciated!
