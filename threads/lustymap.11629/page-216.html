    Click to expand...
    what do you mean "start run"? If you can't close the map you changed the config or it is being changed while it's loaded. That's fixed by "resetmap"
    or if complex map is disabled, "resetmap" and "oxide.unload LustyMap" , "oxide.load LustyMap"/ oxide.reload LustyMap.
    You can also close the Main map by typing /map in game chat.
