    Thanks for the effort, unfortunately the downloading of the map step is incorrect. You should not use the download image link from rustio as it creates a map with the wrong dimensions, if you use that the indicator of where you are on the map will be incorrect, and is very noticeable if you use the zoomed in minimap.
