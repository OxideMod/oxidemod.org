    In this order:

    Check or request for your map+seed to be generated: Beancan - Login
    Remove Lustymap, data and the config.
    Reinstall Lustymap & the data (New icon added)
    Wait for your map to be generated, once complete
    Type resetmap in console and possibly reconnect. (Resetting the map will cause lag for approx 5 seconds)
