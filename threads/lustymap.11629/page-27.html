    First you must download LustyMap.cs file. This one you have to copy to the \server\plugins-folder. Then download the LustyMap.zip-file and extract it. On your server-adress http://YourServerIP:Port/map.jpg you can save your servers map (save as map.jpg). Copy your map into the LustyMap-Folder, that you have extracted and overwrite the existing file. Then copy the Lusty-Map Folder in the \server\Data-Folder. Run your server, then you have a config-file for LustyMap, which you can edit (in the \server\config-folder). After you have edited the config-file you must restart your server or type in the servers-console: oxide.reload LustyMap. That's all. You don't must edit the config-file that the plugin works right.
