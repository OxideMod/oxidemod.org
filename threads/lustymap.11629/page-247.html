  9. Trippy

    gaudeee said: ↑
    still waiting sice 2 hours after wipe

    | [LustyMap] [Warning] Your map is still in the queue to be generated. Checking again in 10 seconds
    Click to expand...
    Unload lusty map. Go to beancan.io, generate the map that you want by hand, download it as map.jpg or map.png. then, upload that image to your servers /oxide/data/lustymap folder. Go to your lusty config, enable custom map. Put the custom map file name as "map.jpg" or whatever the file extension is for that image. Reload lusty map, problem solved. Beancan does this here and there, just remember this for the future!
