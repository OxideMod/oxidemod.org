    Lu6Fer said: ↑
    I also followed the instructions of Wolf, the map is displayed but after 2 or 3 munites it disappears.

    Click to expand...
    In line 373 of LustyMap.cs you will find the line :

    if (lastMoveTime == 90)
    {
    afkDisabled = true;
    DisableUser();
    }

    You can change the number to any number in seconds until you want the map to hide. To turn of this feature you can delete this paragraph. I dont recommend that, because loading the map for afk-players is not worth. I changed it to 3600 wich means it will turn of the map after one hour of no movement.
