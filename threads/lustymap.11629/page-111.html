  9. Venomous

    Okay something is really wrong here. I can't post or the plugin's forum isn't updating properly.

    Anyone figure out how to get playrust.io map.jpg up again without the grid? PlayrustHQ is offline. He took the site down, so no one can generate maps there for an accurate image an longer.

    Edit:
    Okay, I figured it out.

    1. Go to your map for your server in PlayRust.io

    2. Hold Cntrl+Shift+I for inspect, or right click on the header, and click on inspect at the bottom.

    3. At the top of the inspection window, you'll see a tab for sources, click it.

    4. Under sources, you'll see the IP and port for your sever, with a picture of a cloud, click the arrow to see the dropdown for it, it takes you to a preview of map.jpg

    5. The preview will be broken (you do not have authorization from their website to view the source), but, you can right click on the broken icon, and click "open image in new tab."

    6. Once there, your raw map without icons will be under the sources tab, the playrust.io cloud icon, and will say something like map.jpg?level=Procedural%20Map&size=4000&seed=4568. If you click on it, it will now generate an apporpriate preview of your map, which you can right click, "open in new tab," or "save."
