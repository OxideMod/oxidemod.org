    SkullCrusher - Add some extra features to the crushing of human skulls

    This plugin adds new features to the action of crushing another players skull.
    • When you crush a players skull, a message will be printed in chat showing that the player has crushed the other players skull. This was intended to give players an extra way of showing that they dominated the other player
