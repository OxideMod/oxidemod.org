    Item Reskinner - Allows players to reskin crafted items with skins they own.

    This plugin works on an on-craft basis. Basically, when a player crafts an item with an unique skin, the mod recognizes that they own it and adds it to their personal list. I am working on integrating the mod with Steam to pull all owned skins at once, but so far haven't been able to find out how. If you have any knowledge about do this, please contact me.
