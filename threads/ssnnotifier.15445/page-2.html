  7. Umlaut

    Umlaut updated SSNNotifier with a new update entry:

    0.0.5

    Was added commands:
    • /players [filter] [number of rows]
    • /mute alias reason
    • /unmute alias
    • /mutes [filter]
    • /ban alias reason
    • /unban alias
    • /bans [filter]
    Now you can ban and mute players and information about this will be send to our server. All players has own profile with information about bans and mutes.

    alias - it is steamid64 of player or number of list item (/players, /mutes, /bans).
    Example:

    > /players
    1) Player1 2) Player2 3) Player3
    > /ban 2...
