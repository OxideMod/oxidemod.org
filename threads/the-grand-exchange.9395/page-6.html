  14. Scorpyon

    Scorpyon updated The Grand Exchange with a new update entry:

    Feature - Trade Masters

    If you're feeling slightly put off by the idea of going through and updating all the prices for your Exchange, why not enlist some help!

    /addtrademaster <player name> - Adds this player as a trademaster
    /viewtrademasters - Lists all the trademasters on the server

    Add a new trade master and they will have all-access to the Grand Exchange (without needing admin permission).
    Make sure it's someone you trust, of course - as this includes access to price changes, percentages, etc etc...
