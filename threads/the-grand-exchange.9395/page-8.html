    Scorpyon updated The Grand Exchange with a new update entry:

    New Feature - Player Owned Shops

    Ok, this is a biggie!

    Player Owned Shops Added!
    You can now set up your own shop for personal selling (aside from the Grand Exchange)!

    Instructions
    /shophelp - List all shop commands
    /shop - View the current shop you are visiting.
    /myshop - View your own shop. (Must be in the area of your shop)
    /addshopmarker - Add a marker to create your own shop (Max size of shop - 13x13 blocks)
    /removeshopmarkers - Remove any markers you have made...
