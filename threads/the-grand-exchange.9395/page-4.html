    Ah, I see. I used two markers (for now) for two reasons:
    1. It guarantees a rectangle area which is easier mathematically (for the moment) to calculate when a player is in the right area. When you start getting strange shaped areas, the maths becomes exponentially more tricky to work with. I will be trying to enable multiple markers at some point, however.
    2. This was more of a prototype / proof of concept to see if it works and people like the idea. I didn't want to invest too much time in a complicated marker system if people weren't bothered by it.

    Also: Oops! Use /markadd , not /marktrade! (My mistake there! - I changed the command and forgot to update)
