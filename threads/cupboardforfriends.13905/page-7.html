    That error would propably still appear due to the following reason:
    Cupboard for Friends checks for FriendsAPI on load - meaning if FriendsAPI is NOT loaded when Cupboard for Friends is, it will throw that error.
    As FriendsAPI is loaded AFTER Cupboard for Friends (Alphabetical Load Order), it will always error. Though once FriendsAPI is loaded, Cupboard for Friends will detect that and work with FriendsAPI.
