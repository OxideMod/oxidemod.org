  1. FuJiCuRa

    Fujikura submitted a new resource:

    No Weapon Drop - Prevents dropping of active weapon on wounded or headshot/instant-kill

    This small plugin prevents players from dropping their active weapon once they get into wounded state. This also includes the death state after being wounded.
    Furthermore it prevents players from dropping their active weapon on instant-kill/headshot.
