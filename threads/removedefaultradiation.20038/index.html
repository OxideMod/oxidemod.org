    Remove Default Radiation - Removes default radiation zones, for use with plugins that enable server radiation

    This will destroy all radiation zones on the map so you are free to use any plugin that use's radiation, without the worry of a few uncontrollable high radiation areas.

    First off. If you think that's hard. You have more Surprises sir.
    So you have your .cs plugin/file. You need to go into your server. Go to Oxide then plugins. At the top bar you see; UPLOAD> you click on that. Click on; Choose File and it will take you to your Downloads Folder. There. You Dbl Click it. It will load into your server. It's also Best to have your SERVER OFF when you do this. Once you have loaded ALL your .cs files/plugingins that you want. You can now restart your server. Let it Fully load completely. While it is loading. Go to your "log Viewer" and click on Stream File. The Line that is: eg(oxide/logs/oxide_2017-08-31.txt). Here you will see your server loading itself with all your plugins. Right now it is making your .json files for you which you can configer each mod to your liking.
    When it has loaded and said Fully Installed. You can now go to your config Folder the same way as your plugins Folder. eg (Oxide/config.) When you are there you will see all the mods you can configuer. You want to click on the ball to the right of your mod and it will say DownLoad). Click on all your .json's the same way. By doing this. It will place them in your DownLoads Folder for you. Then you can adjust them there by left clicking to Highlight it. And click the right to bring up a window. You want to go to and click on "Edit with Notepad or Notepad++. It will show you all the contents in the .json. Read each Developers guidelines and Follow. After you have done one or All of your .json's. STOP your Server. Go to your Oxide/config Folder again and DELETE all of the mods you have touched. At the Top you will see upload. Do the same for your New .json's as the way you installed your plugins. Restart your Server. Check your Logs again to make sure everything is loading fine and no conflicts. If not. Then you are finished! Make sure you keep them Updated when a new Update comes out. BUT!!! You only have to DELETE the .cs Files only. Because the Updates are only the .cs files.
