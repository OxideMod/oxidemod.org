  18. Bamabo

    Mikeyl said: ↑
    Hi how do i make so when a player has been online for 1 hour they can get into Player group instead of New-Player?
    Click to expand...
    /loyalty addg 60 Player Should have you covered there. If New-Player is being assigned through Loyalty too then the player will automatically be removed from New-Player upon getting the rank of Player. If it's not however, you can simply add another entry that removes that rank. /loyalty addg 60 -New-Player like so. Notice the '-' before the ranks name. That will have it revoked when reaching the set amount of loyalty instead of granted.
