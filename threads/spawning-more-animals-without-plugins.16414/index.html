  16. Diesel 42o

    Since the latest update these are available to be set via RCON commands:

    find .population in RCON

    bear.population Population active on the server, per square km (2)
    boar.population Population active on the server, per square km (4)
    chicken.population Population active on the server, per square km (3)
    horse.population Population active on the server, per square km (3)
    stag.population Population active on the server, per square km (3)
    wolf.population Population active on the server, per square km (2)
    Click to expand...
    Perhaps turning these up will spawn in more animals per square km?
