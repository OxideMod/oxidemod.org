    Hello,

    It's been known for a while now but I totally forgot so I thought I would share what was happening earlier today on my server. We were getting massive FPS drops on the server to a point where it was not playable anymore, after multiple restarts it kept happening until the guy slipped up... Basically a group of 6-7 players were using instacraft to make thousands of spears and they over flow onto the floor eventually crashing your server.

    I know this is a well known attack but it's just a reminder for people who are unaware.

    If you use instacraft do not allow it on -

    Code:
    Spears
Swords
Machetes(All the items that drop with no bag entity)
