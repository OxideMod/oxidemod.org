    JoeSheep submitted a new resource:

    KDGUI - Displays your kills and deaths as an extension to your hotbar!



    KDGUI displays your kills and deaths as an extension to the hotbar and resets when you log off! Only player kills count and you don't get extra kills for killing yourself!

    _____________________________________________________
    Permissions:
    kdgui.show
    All players with this permission will have the GUI show and have access to the command to toggle the GUI.
