Nfo has proven to me that they cannot handle these attacks. A couple days ago the node that my server was hosted on was null-routed. We lost an insane amount of players. Under Nfo the intial DDoS attacks on the server were easily migated and caused about a minute of lag at the worst. After two weeks of attacks they became more powerful and, at first, hit off around half of the players and then eventually everyone for a moment. After the null-route we knew we had to switch. It is extremely sad to see all these server owners go through this especially after building up their communities. I was fortunate to receive enough donations to be able to afford such an expensive host but with the lack of players that now are online it only seems a matter of time before this game server dies out. We may be forced to look at alternatives like Minecraft, ARK, ArmA, Gmod or whatever doesn't look like it sucks at the moment. On a different subject: "teaming" up with other communities may not be a bad idea for the time being but it doesn't necessarily fix these attacks. They only way around them is to have a box with ridiculously good DDoS protection... Which would actually allow a lot of your servers to show up on the server list again and get back on track.
