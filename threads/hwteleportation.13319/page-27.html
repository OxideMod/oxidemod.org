  15. LaserHydra

    DouYuTV_一明 said: ↑
    You don't have permission to use this command.
    Click to expand...
    You need to grant the people you like to have access to specific sections of the plugin the permission for that section.

    Permissions:
    • teleportation.admin for admin commands (/tp & /tphere)
    • teleportation.tpr for teleport request commands (/tpr & /tpa)
    • teleportation.home for home commands (/home, /sethome, /removehome & /homes)
    How permissions work:
    • /grant user <player> <permission> grant permission to player
    • /grant group <group> <permission> grant permission to group
     
    LaserHydra, Jan 13, 2016
