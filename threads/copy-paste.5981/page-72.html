  18. Evilyn Corporated

    Fox ☠ said: ↑
    when i copy my shop and npc in the shop i cant copy can can i enable somethink in the config or no ?
    Click to expand...
    No you can't. You'll need to re-create your NPC in the pasted shop. We gave up on NPCs in shops as it's such a pain to have to handle after every wipe, and we now use the Rewards Store (/s) instead.

    Something we tried with NPCs in shops is to:-

    #1 - First create a new one in the newly pasted shop where we wanted them to stand so we had the correct co-ordinates for them.
    #2 - Unload the plugin.
    #3 - Edit the NPC .json data manually (take a copy first!) and copy the co-ordinates onto the old NPC, and then delete the new NPC from the data.
    #4 - Load the NPC plugin.

    If you've got it right, your shop NPC will be standing in the location of the new one you created, and the new one will be gone.

    It's a pain. We gave up on it, you may decide to do the same.
     
    Evilyn Corporated, Jun 9, 2017
