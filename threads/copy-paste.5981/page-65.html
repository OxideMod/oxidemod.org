  15. Gary Yates

    Oooook. Well I figured where I had went wrong and why I was getting only half a building. The reason was I built the foundations using /build plugin. I hasnt realised though that I was building a little too close to the ground and once the building as pictured above was completed I copied it. I wouldnt copy the foundation placed by /build as it would never have been allowed while building normally. This meant that it would always copy only the legal foundations. Meaning when I /paste it pasted what it copied and all supporting building above the illegal foundation was not pasted as it was never copied. I hope that makes sense anyway. Moral of the Story. Build properly on flat surfaces!
