  19. Evilyn Corporated

    -^kHz *G* said: ↑
    Hi, can i copy paste my building from fortify into my own server? And how do i do it?

    I can't find any video on youtube or google about it?
    Click to expand...
    Yes. Create or load your Fortify base. Click the menu button and then select the Rust copy-paste checkbox. The save/load dialogue will turn red to let you know you're now in copy-paste json mode. Save your base and it will save as a json file. Add it to the \oxide\data\copypaste\ directory on your Rust server and you can then use it on your server.

    Oh. And it works the other way too.

    One proviso, sometimes a wall will be the wrong way round and you'll need to rotate it. Other than that it's a huge time saver.
