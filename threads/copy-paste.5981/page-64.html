  10. ChemoZabe

    WADDS said: ↑
    Please help... What am I doing wrong? I copied my base then when I paste it back somewhere half is above the ground and the other is under ground when you go noclip.
    Thank you
    Click to expand...
    Use the height argument in your paste ("/paste BUILDINGNAME height XX"). The plugin always tries to put your lowest base at sea level (zero height) and if you paste it at a higher elevation, you need to adjust it. Just takes a bit of trial and error to get it right, use "/undo" if it's not good and add or subtract to the height ## until you get it perfect. Or if you are pasting it in the same place on the same world seed, use "/placeback BUILDINGNAME" instead to put it back excactly where it was.
     
    ChemoZabe, Apr 30, 2017
