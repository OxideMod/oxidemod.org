  5. DylanSMR

    For yall that enjoy this plugin, the new version is in the works. Not a whole lot of new features, mostly reworked. The UI will be completely simplified and remodeled using Image Library for a more security like feel as meant by this plugin. I will probably be changing the opening of doors to just unlocking and locking them. To better match our real life situation. Turrets and all of that will be the exact same allowing you to turn off and on furnaces. I am also reworking how I grab objects from the world to better use the resources available. Before I used a check on each location and searched for the entity. I have decided to use each entities net id(which is different between each entity). Using a more simple search will better use resources allowing for a smoother experience. Of course all of this is roughly one or two weeks away. Maybe less depending on how much time I have to work on the plugin. Thanks for your patience and my I must apologize negligence about fully supporting this plugin.
