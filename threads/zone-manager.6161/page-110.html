    It's possible:

    pvpgod true/false => all players have PVP god mode in the zone
    pvegod true/false => all players have PVE god mode in the zone

    --

    enter_message "XXXXX"/false => set a message that will be sent to players when they enter the zone (You're not entering a player vs player free zone)
    leave_message "XXXXXX"/false=> set a message that will be sent to players when they leave the zone (Be careful out there.. some of the people don't welcome strangers)

    You'd have to figure out the best way to cut your map apart though.
