  20. Steenamaroo

    You could make a zone covering half of the map and set 'pvpgod true' in that zone.
    The people in there would be protected.

    For a straight line border, go to the middle of your PVPGod section and create a new zone.
    Once created, do /zone size "** ** **"

    I've no idea what size half a map is but just start somewhere arbitrary..maybe "10000 200 10000"

    Now fly to the edge and resize the zone (with the same command) to make it bigger/smaller as needed.
