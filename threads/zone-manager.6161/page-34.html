  5. Quicken

    Dead Skin Mask said: ↑
    Ok, im kinda lost. I want to make a town. I want people to be able to gather and live. I dont want pvp available in the zone and do not want teleporting available in that zone either. How do i do that?
    Click to expand...
    I created such a town "Rust Town" here are the zone commands I used:

    --- Zone Rust Town ----
    /zone_add nobuild true nodeploy true nosuicide true name "Rust Town"
    /zone radius 60 undestr true noplayerloot true nosuicide true
    /zone notp true

    I Then created the following additional zone messages while in the zone. and walked around to test the edges of the zone (60 is about 10 foundations long, I think 1 foundation is 3 units)

    /zone enter_message "Welcome to Rust Town"
    /zone leave_message "Your are leaving the safety of Rust Town"
