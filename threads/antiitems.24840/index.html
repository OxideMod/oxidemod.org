    redBDGR submitted a new resource:

    AntiComponents - Remove the need for certain items in crafting and repairing

    This plugin gives you the option to completely remove the need for having some items for crafting or repairing.

    This basically works by adding items to hidden item slots in the players inventory, so make sure you choose your items carefully.

    Permissions:
    • anticomponents.use - players with this will receive the benefits of this plugin
