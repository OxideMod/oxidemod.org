  1. Raptor007

    Raptor007 submitted a new resource:

    Wanted For Murder - Players can punish unwanted PvP.

    This provides a disincentive for PvP without actually removing the ability to kill. If you kill a sleeping player, or kill an awake player and then they type /punish in chat, you will be branded a murderer. Wanted murderers can't have any beds or sleeping bags, so death has consequences if you're wanted.

  8. Raptor007

    Not if both players are awake, but it will automatically tag murderers if they kill someone sleeping.

    I could pretty easily add an option to do this for awake victims too, but keep in mind that as soon as someone is branded a murderer, their sleeping bags and beds immediately disappear. Typing /forgive doesn't bring those back. So I think it makes more sense to wait until the victim types /punish if they're awake.
