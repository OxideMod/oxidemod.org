  1. k1lly0u

    k1lly0u submitted a new resource:

    Restore Upon Death - Restore player inventorys on death and remove the items from their corpse. Configurable loss amount

    Save player inventory's when they die and remove the items from the corpse. Adjust the percentage of items that should be left behind (default 25%). When the player respawns they will be given their gear back minus the lost percentage. This plugin will save data between restarts, so sleepers are also protected.

    Config
    Code:
    {
  "PercentageOfItemsLost": 25
}
