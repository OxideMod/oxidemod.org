  15. Phoenix Renegade

    Wulf said: ↑
    I am the plugin creator, I just haven't worked on this one in awhile so I can't recall for sure. I know it needs some work though.
    Click to expand...
    I actually found out that the "cannot be looted" permission works despite having /god toggled on or off. It doesn't matter. If that permission is linked to the group or user, that person is always not able to be looted, period. Taking that tag off seemed to remove the issue. I had to reassign each permission separately, omitting the not lootable one. So, it serves its purpose, certainly. You just need to assign each individually because not all of them toggle, so unless you always want each of the permissions active all the time, you shouldn't use the "godmode.*" permission.
     
    Phoenix Renegade, Jul 24, 2017
