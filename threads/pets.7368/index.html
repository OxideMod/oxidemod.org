    Bombardir submitted a new resource:

    NPC Controller - controll any npc (not player)

    It's very very alpha, so there will be a lot of bugs now, but it's working!
    How to:
    1. /npc - activate (auth lvl 2)
    2. look at NPC and press USE button
    • To attack other npc or player look at him and press USE
    • To use follow/unfollow command look at your npc and press USE
    P.S. If u want to change NPC, then reload plugin or kill old npc.
