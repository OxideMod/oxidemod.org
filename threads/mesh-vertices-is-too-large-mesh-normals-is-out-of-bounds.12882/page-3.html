  15. sqroot

    Right now the only thing you can do is make sure the entity count doesn't increase too much, as I am pretty sure that roughly the amount of times this error appears is relative to how much players are building and how many entities there are.
    Disable NoDecay, reduce gather rates, increase building costs, eventually even speed up decay, etc.
    I've found that those bases with many layers of walls also cause this error a lot. Again, to avoid this, don't use NoDecay.
    It's not a nice solution, but sadly you can't do much about it right now.
