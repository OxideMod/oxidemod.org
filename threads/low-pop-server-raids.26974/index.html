  2. clamchowder1314

    I've experimented with a 2.5k map size on a 2x server. Players generally enjoy it more for the PvP aspect. But I recently changed it to 3k size map and it's doing much better.

    Reasonings:
    Think about it this way, image you are the player and get raided on a server. What would you do? Build a new base? Of course not, generally players don't enjoy rebuilding and restarting from the beginning on the same "Old" server.

    You have to take in count that majority of the content creators of rust on youtube and twitch are vanilla-encouraged by the media. Meaning people are less likely to take a server serious on a 2x, not to mention how much less they would care for a server that's a 5x server.

    Personal Reasons when joining a 5x server or more: It's just really a server I sit on to mess around and not care about anything after I get raided on the main server. That's what's always been on player's minds from my past 5000 hours of rust gaming. Now that I am hosting a server I can even understand and relate to.

    Things you have to take account into: Why are rustified and other old vanilla servers so popular and never die? They've been the same exact thing since the beginning of rust. I mean, it's vanilla! Right? Since they've existed from the start of the rust community it's the foundation of all rust youtubers/twitch streamers playground. They make all the videos and well edited contents on those servers based on that. So in order to maintain a good population and a fair amount of "Loyal players". Because who cares about the 100 players that you get who leaves and never comes back after the first day of fame right?

    You might be wondering, why am I writing this. I was just bored really, I hold a server of my own on average of 40-30 players during the afternoons on my timezone. It's nothing big, but I've been receiving donations enough to maintain and seek for customized plugins for the server hence which brings some sort of value for you to read through my suggestions.

    Edit: The whole point of this is to tell you that you have to be unique in your own ways. Not just some 5x server anyone can create. Try to think outside the box to seek for something else that might capture people's attention. IMPORTANT, you first need some sort of stable 10 player fanbase who comes on daily. All the things you might of thought of will not work just because it's been overused such as, events, plugins for arenas, ridiculous kits for players from the start. Flooding the server with excessive amount of plugins which will never work. Good luck
