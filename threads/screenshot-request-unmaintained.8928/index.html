    Spicy submitted a new resource:

    Screenshot Request - Makes screenshot testing a hell of a lot easier for admins.

    Makes screenshot testing really easy for admins by telling the player what to do and enable branding on their client just by typing in one command.
    Code:
    Oxide permission: "screenshotrequest"
oxide.grant user <name> screenshotrequest
oxide.revoke user <name> screenshotrequest
    Command is: /sr <name>


    I hope you all enjoy my plugin!

  15. Spicy

    Tsei said: ↑
    If the date from of the top right corner differs from the may 1st 2015, (E.g. 1st 2014), does it automatically mean he is using cheats or posting old screenshot?
    Click to expand...
    Most of the time, if the date is different, they will be using cheats. Common dates to look out for are 20th July 2014 and 6th May 2015 which come from the 2 most popular cheats. You may see some other dates which will indicate cheating but these are the most common.

    There are a few dates to look out for, on the other hand, that show legitimacy in the player:

    - 22nd April 2015
    - 2nd May 2015

    The 22nd April 2015 one is for players who have not updated Rust because they see no use for the update as it doesn't change anything, therefore they have the version before that, compiled on the 22nd April 2015.

    The 2nd May 2015 date is simply related to time zones in places such as New Zealand.

    Hope this helped!
