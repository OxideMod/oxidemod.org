    No Signs - Prevents players from using signs on the server

    This plugin prevents players from using signs on the server.
    • Removes all signs from the map when loaded
    • Disables crafting of signs
    • Removes signs from a players inventory when picked up
    • Destroys signs if they are placed by a player
    • Allows admins to use signs temporarily (they will be removed from the map when the plugin is reloaded)
    Development of this plugin has kindly funded by Trust.

  5. bawNg

    cenk said: ↑
    just wondering why need such plugin?
    Click to expand...
    In addition to making skybridges possible, signs are also badly optimized at the moment and have bugs which crash players games.

    This plugin was requested mainly due to performance issues. Players experience bad FPS due to a lot of signs, players games can freeze when signs are spawned, and many players also have serious issues where their game crashes every time a sign comes into their view. Admins are also unable to spectate due to these crashes.
