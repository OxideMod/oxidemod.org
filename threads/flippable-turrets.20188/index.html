    DylanSMR submitted a new resource:

    Flippable Turrets - Allows you to flip a turret (attach to a roof)

    This plugin simply allows you to detach/attach a turret to a roof.

    Commands:
    • /flipturret - This flips the turret the player is aiming at.
    • /unflipturret - This unflips the turret the player is aiming at.
    Language:
    Code:
    {"Flipped", "You have flipped that turret successfully."},
{"NotFlipped", "A error occured upon flipping the turret. This might be because its already flipped!"},
{"Unflipped", "You have unflipped that turret successfully"},
