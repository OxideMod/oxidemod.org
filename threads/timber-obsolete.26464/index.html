    Mattparks submitted a new resource:

    Timber - Makes trees and cacti fall before being destroyed.

    Timber is a plugin that causes trees and cacti to fall on your server.

    Timber can be configured from just a visual effect, to a game changing feature, the config file is flexible to your needs. Timber works with GatherManager and other rate management plugins.
