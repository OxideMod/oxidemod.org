  1. Colon Blow

    Colon Blow submitted a new resource:

    Fire Arrows - Players can shoot arrows with fire

    Allows Authorized players the ability to shoot arrows with fire.

    Once player is authorized and has a Bow or Crossbow equipped. They can toggle fire arrows off and on by pressing the mouse wheel button down.

    A fire arrow icon appears at bottom left when its enabled, and goes away when normal arrows are selected. Icon can be not show via config file.



    Permissions:
    firearrows.allowed

    Code:
    oxide.grant <group|user> <name|id>  firearrows.allowed...

  8. Colon Blow

    consumable items required is a go. working on it.

    Also, what about Tiers of fire arrows?
    Tier 1 = Fire Arrow --- arrow as it is now, with a little extra impact Heat Damage. takes cloth and fuel.
    Tier 2 = FireBall Arrow --- bigger effect on impact, a bit more impact Heat damage, bigger fire afterwards. Takes Cloth, Fuel and Crude Oil.
    Tier 3 = FireBomb Arrow --- explosion effect type on impact, a good amount of impact explosion damage, even bigger fireball afterwards. Takes Cloth, Fuel, Crude Oil and Explosivies.

    of course, these Tiers can be turned off/on in configuration file and permissions can be set for each Tier.
     
    Colon Blow, Dec 29, 2015
