  1. Calytic Community Admin Community Mod

    Calytic submitted a new resource:

    NoEscape - Prevent teleport of owner while being raided

    This is an untested proof-of-concept for the purpose of preventing the owner of a house from teleporting (home/tpr) away during a raid of their base (strictly only their base that they themselves constructed).

    Configuration
    distance
    The distance the owner must be from where the raid is occurring (default 100 meters)

    duration
    The length of time before the owner may teleport again (default 50 seconds)
