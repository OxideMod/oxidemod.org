  5. Venomous

    ScHigGn said: ↑
    how to block /tpb form N-Teleportation ?
    /tpa, /town and /home is blocked, but not /tpb
    Click to expand...
    Very strange, any players other than Auth 2 would have that permission. N Teleportation states that it's a permission, and by default only admin have that capability. Try taking nteleportation.tpb away from default group.

    To take it away go to console, type:

    revoke group default nteleportation.tpb

    Then give yourself or your admin permission again:

    grant group admin nteleportation.tpb
    or
    grant user ScHigGn nteleportation.tpb
