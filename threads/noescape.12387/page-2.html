  9. Calytic Community Admin Community Mod

    SannyOK said: ↑
    England, what does clanCheck do, can you explain?
    Click to expand...
    The intended function is to prevent clan-mates from blocking your teleport (say perhaps by accident).

    So if I am "[MyClan] Calytic" and I build a wall. Then "[MyClan] Bob" accidentally throws a survey charge and hits my wall. Before, this would block me from teleporting. Now, if the clanCheck option is set to "true", my teleport will not be blocked because we are in the same clan.

    I added this to make this plugin slightly less annoying. =)
     
    Calytic, Jan 11, 2016
