  5. Calytic Community Admin Community Mod

    serezhadelaet said: ↑
    I mean, if player just shot to structure with AK and 5.56 Explosive Bullets, then RaidBlock will be on.
    Click to expand...
    The way it works is it checks the majority damage type. So if your configuration is for explosive damage only, then explosive bullets will not be counted because the bullet damage is always greater than the explosive damage from said bullet. You may still get Raid Blocked from explosive rounds but I doubt that happens very often.

    I do not plan on adding a system that micro-manages sources of damage. Thanks for the feedback though, it is appreciated.
