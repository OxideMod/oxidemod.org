  1. mvrb

    mvrb submitted a new resource:

    NoSeedGrief - Blocks seed from being planted if Building Blocked. Seeds are refunded.

    It's currently possible to grief players by placing hemp/corn/pumpkin seeds around bases.

    This plugin checks if the player has building privilege when planting seeds. The seeds will be removed and refunded if the player can't build.
