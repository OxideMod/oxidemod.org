  12. [X] Meep

    Type 'dump' in console then navigate to

    /steamapps/common/rust_dedicated/diagnostics/1

    You will see a list of files that give info about colliders, the one you are looking for is called Physics

    Physics Information
    Total Colliders: 150,097
    Total RigidBodys: 122
    Mesh Colliders: 113,570
    Box Colliders: 26,278
    Sphere Colliders: 982
    Capsule Colliders: 9,266

    270,000 total colliders is the limit. Reneb says 265K you will start to see issues.
