    NameRewards - Assign players groups based on phrases found in their name.

    Using NameRewards, you can grant rewards similar to many Counter Strike gambling sites for having a specified phrase in your name. NameRewards allows you to add your players to groups depending on phrases found in their names.

    Example
    For instance, if my name was Kappasaurus | RustySurvival.IO, and RustySurvival.IO was one of my specified phrases, upon joining I would be assigned to the specified group, if I re-logged without "RustySurvival.IO" in my name I would be removed from the...
