  13. NobodyFTW

    dofof37 said: ↑
    Is there a way to exclude some stats so they aren't tracked?
    Click to expand...
    You can either restrict each stat from players with the Restricted To Players setting, or you can do your own whitelist of stats enabling the Enable Rank Whitelist and then setting the stats you want to be shown in the Rank Whitelist, all these in the Settings section of the configuration file. And FYI even though you restrict or make a whitelist of stats, Rank-ME will keep tracking all player stats in the background, so in case you want to enable it back again all the stats are updated.
     
    NobodyFTW, Feb 24, 2016
