  14. NobodyFTW

    SkinN õ.Õ'.|. updated OverThrone with a new update entry:

    OverThrone | King Chat and Prefix!

    [ CHANGELOG ]
    • Added plugin Resource Id
    • Added King chat prefix
    • Added a wipe check on Init, checks if the King persistence does not exist and restarts the system
    • Removed obsolete key from the database
    • Fixed error on /king command when trying to get player name of a NoneType value
    - This update adds new settings and values to the configuration file, no resets are required.
    - This version may be incompatible with Chat Handler plugin.
    Click to expand...
    For now on OverThrone may be incompatible with Chat Handler, and perhaps more plugins in the future. I will only focus on adding OverThrone's features regardless of what other plugins features do. I am sorry about any inconvenience on this matter.


    [DOUBLEPOST=1435277652][/DOUBLEPOST][ ALPHA - 0.0.3 - The KingsMen Update ]
    The next update will be totally focused on KingsMen, or Royal Guard if you will. The King will be able to add/remove any player they want the his Royal Guard, to protect him when he needs the most. I will also try to make it compatible with Rust:IO Clans, so once you are the King your whole clan automatically becomes your Royal Guard, so you can save time on adding each one by one. And even if you rather remove someone of the clan you should be able to.
    We will still work on other ideas on where the Royal Guard can be useful, and and what other roles they can play on the kingdom. And of course as always we count on suggestions to help us develop these ideas.
