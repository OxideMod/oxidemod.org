  17. NobodyFTW

    Dreke said: ↑
    When i kill animals, why don't i see where i shot the animal at anymore but it does when players are killed, I checked the config but i don't see the option to turn it on if possible.
    Click to expand...
    This is an optional name format you can add to your messages.

    EXP:
    With body parts:
    '{attacker} killed a {victim} hitting the {bodypart}.'
    Without body parts:
    '{attacker} killed a {victim} with a {weapon} from {distance}m.'
    Extra example:
    '{attacker} killed a {victim} from {distance}m and it's the {bodypart}.'

    You can move name formats or even remove them as you'd like, it's really up to you how you want your messages to be.
