    Absolut submitted a new resource:

    Factions - Allows players to join pre-made factions which provides protection

    Provides a three Faction system that does not require being Steam Friends or any other external plugins/mods. Players join the server and are prompted to join a faction. Joining a faction prevents FF and also prevents destruction of Faction buildings(unless authorized on a nearby ToolCupboard). There is in faction chat that only broadcasts to other faction members. There is built in Death Notifications. There is support for giving Tokens, Economics, or Rewards Points on player kills (this...
