  1. Absolut

    I just implemented a Challenge system that allows a faction member to challenge the leader. If a player challenges the leader it gives a 15 minute warning to the player and the leader. After that time, it allows FF and Building destruction between those two players. If the challenger kills the leader, the leader is set back to 0 playtime and the next player up becomes leader. If the Leader wins, the challenger is removed from the faction. If the player logs off during the challenge it ends. If the leader logs off during the challenge it does NOT end.

    I need some testers. Let me know (keep in mind the version you will test also have several other changes). Thanks!
