    Change the numbers in the LootTable to a higher value...

    "ammo.pistol": 100,
    "ammo.pistol.hv": 60,
    "ammo.pistol.fire": 22,
    "ammo.rifle": 300,
    "ammo.rifle.explosive": 60,
    "ammo.rifle.hv": 160,
    "ammo.rifle.incendiary": 40
    ^^^^^^^^^^^^^
    Example... Put however much you want.
