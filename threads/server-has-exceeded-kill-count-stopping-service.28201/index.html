  10. v1ruspr0ne

    @Melvin_A51 I posted in this thread previously as we had the same issue, however we resolved our issue easily by changing host. Now we own a hosting company instead lol.

    However the issue is caused by your host limiting the amount of RAM on the server. Upgrading the slots for the server is not a fix, and changing to a smaller map is only a very short term fix.

    My advice is to change your host to someone that is not going to limit the RAM for your server. Our own servers Run at a mimimum of 4.5gb RAM and range up to 12-15gb RAM depending on playercount, plugins, and mapsize.

    There are many hosts i could recommend including ourselves, but it is ultimately your own choice to do this.

    I wish you luck and hope you find something better.
