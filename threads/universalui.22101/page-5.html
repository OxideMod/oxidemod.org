  5. Tovarisch_Jay

    ™War Is Real™ said: ↑
    How i can have only 2 pages per section? default is 3 but i want have 2 how i can do that?
    Click to expand...
    Use a json editor such as www.jsoneditoronline.org. It will make it so much easier to modify the config.

    Using this website as an example, copy your entire config to the window on the left.
    In the middle, click the arrow that points to the right, this will fill up the window on the right with some information.
    Click the black arrow next to "sections",
    then the black arrow next to the section you want to modify,
    then the black arrow next to pages.

    This will show you the number of pages you'll have for that section.
    To remove one of those, click the square to the left of the page you want to remove and select "remove". (To the right of the 6 dots.)

    Once removed, in the middle again, click the black arrow that points to the left, this will modify the config on the left with what you've changed.
    Select all the text in the left window, copy and paste that back to your .json file. Save and upload.
