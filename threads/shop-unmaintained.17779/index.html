    Shop - Simple shop and reward system

    If player kills zombies or animals, he will get the money (one game dollar).

    Commands:
    • /money - show your money (all)
    • /buy <item> <value> - buy item (all)
    • /sendmoney <nickname> <value> - give money to player (all)
    • /shop - show list of item (all)
    • /shopadd <itemname> <money> - add item to shop list (Admin)
    • /shopadel <itemname> - del item in shop list (Admin)
