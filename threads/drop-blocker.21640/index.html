  13. Slut

    DikkatDeli said: ↑
    can you please more spesific for this part " edit MagicCraft and delete the full inventory line "
    Click to expand...
    MagicCraft utilises it's own Full Inventory feature when upon crafting, it takes the items, checks for full inv then refunds your items if your inventory is full, but the problem with that is that DropBlocker doesn't actually take the items and refunds them, it just simply blocks it therefore MagicCraft refunds resources when the resources weren't consumed in the first place thus resulting in a dupe glitch. (It gets really bad when you try to max craft a stack of gunpowder as you get a lot of sulfur out of it.)

    Now how to fix it.

    In Magic Craft delete lines #126-134 #145 #149-158
     
    Slut, Oct 30, 2016
