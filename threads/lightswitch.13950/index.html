    Kodfod submitted a new resource:

    Light Switch - Allows you to turn on and off all the lights on the server.

    Commands:
    • /lights <on/off> -Will turn on or off all the torches on the server.
    • /lights <on/off> [radius] - Will turn on or off torches in a radius
    Permissions:
    • lightswitch.switch
    Please note: Lights can only be turned on during the night. This will NOT give you the ability to turn torches on during the day.
