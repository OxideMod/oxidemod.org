    I don't think you actually understand what he's saying, so it'd be good if you started reading what people are saying.
    Godmode provides its own god mode implementation, in addition to the Rust god mode.
    He wants to completly remove the god mode of an admin, completly independently of whether he's using the god mode provided by Rust or the god mode provided by the godmode plugin.
    Since "god" is a client command, I'm fairly sure that what he's asking isn't possible.
    To achieve this, he'd have to remove the rights of his admins/mods and then grant them the canusegodmode permission in a special group, which is probably not what he wants, because this would also take away their other rights.
