  12. LaserHydra

    AluhuHadezz! said: ↑
    how do i add the command then??
    Click to expand...
    Console Command:
    grant user Aluhu backpack.use (to grant yourself the permission)
    or / and
    grant group admin backpack.use (to grant the permission to the admin group)
    or / and
    grant group player backpack.use (to grant the permission to the player group)
