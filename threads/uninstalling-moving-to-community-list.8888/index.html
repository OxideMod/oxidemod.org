  7. PaiN

    I had a server hosted by GameServers...

    How to Wipe:
    Go to your server panel and tick the box where it says "Full Wipe" and the press "Reset" button near it .... it will make a new server without oxide...

    How to add Oxide:
    Go to your Server's panel again and go to the label where it says Mods and Press the button "Install" Near the Oxide 2 download

    How to update Oxide:
    If you had your Oxide installed before then just go to Mods label and press "Install" near the Oxide 2 download

    How to update Server:
    I prefer making the "How to Wipe" Option but for normal update go to your Mods Label and near the "Update Server" press "Install"
