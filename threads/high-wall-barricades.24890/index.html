  1. Guilty Spark

    Guilty Spark submitted a new resource:

    High Wall Barricades - Automatically removes High External Walls placed outside of cupboard range after a period of time.

    A lot of servers get cluttered by High External Walls that are used as temporary barricades during fighting; this plug-in automatically weakens and later removes those barricades.

    When a High External Wall is placed outside of cupboard range its health is automatically reduced to a percentage of its maximum health and it will be removed after a certain amount of time unless someone repairs it.

  8. Guilty Spark

    MansurBey said: ↑
    working good

    i set remove time: 300 and placed a high High External Wall before server restart. Since 50 minutes not removed.

    Can you fix?
    Click to expand...
    Unfortunately due to the low-impact method used to remove the walls, when the server restarts any walls pending removal are left to be removed by normal decay - this shouldn't happen often unless you're restarting your server often and even then the walls should fully decay much sooner than they normally would given their low starting health. I will see if there's any elegant solution to fix this other than removing all walls outside of cupboard range on server restarts.

    And yes, this plug-in is only for High External Walls.
