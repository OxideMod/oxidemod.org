  9. Colon Blow

    Still working on it
    So far this is what I have working:

    - typing /copter puts player in noclip (if not already) and then copter attaches to player. it then goes up about 10 ft.
    - copter has a 10ft ground proximity. no lower, it will force player to the 10ft.
    - typing /landcopter will lower copter to ground and remove copter, then removing player from noclip.
    - copter currently uses a Battery system that does have limited charge (limits range) with onscreen indicator of remaining charge. (because this is rust. and combining high voltage electricity and barrels loaded with fuel and fire are always a good thing)
    - the faster you move, the more battery drains. noclipping and using sprint will drain it fast.
    - copter can be recharged by getting close to any substation around the map, then it will start to charge if you stay still and show indicator. (dangerous as they are normally close to rad towns)
    - running out of charge while flying will result in rotor stopping and copter going to ground and blowing up.
    - pilot can use /dropnet to drop / raise a long cargo netting to ground (from 10ft limit) to allow players to climb up and give player stuff.
    - pilot can use /dropbomb to drop one of two barrel bombs on the back of copter.
    - pilot can use /reloadcopter to reload one barrel bomb, if player is within 10ft of a Red Rad Barrel. can be used to reload both.

    Only limitation now is player has to be able to use noclip. Moving a player without this seems make antihack very mad and even turning it off tends to be not near as smooth. (for now, testing some new player movement coding soon).
    And players cannot just hang on netting right now. they will fall off if copter moves away.


    anyone have any ideas? suggestions?
