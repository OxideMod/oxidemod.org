  7. Steenamaroo

    Yeah, just make a kit that you'd like your bot to have, save it, make it hidden to your players if you like, then assign it to the bot in config/data.

    Weapons go in hotbar belt, attire goes in attire bar, and the main container can be used for any extra items you'd like, as loot items for your players.
