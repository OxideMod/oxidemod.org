  11. Steenamaroo

    So, Can you explain exactly what is the difference between True/False for the murderer setting ?
    Do they move away from their preset zone/radius, is their kit altered in any way, or their weapons ?
    Click to expand...
    Murderer is a new prefab introduced into Rust this week.
    Where the scientist is an AI with Hazmat suit and mp5/shotgun, the Murderer is a running 'zombie' character with a melee weapon.

    Setting Murder:True means your bots will be the latter type.
    Setting it false means the plugin will act as it did prior to the update.
    I suggest always having kit set to 'default' when using Murderer:True.
