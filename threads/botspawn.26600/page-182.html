  17. Steenamaroo

    TheStoffie said: ↑
    "Calling hook OnNpcPlayerTarget resulted in a conflict between the following plugins: ZoneManager - False (Boolean), BotSpawn (True (Boolean))"
    Click to expand...
    Hi,
    You can safely ignore this. It's not an error so much as a notification.
    It's telling you that one plug says it's Ok for a bot to attack you, and another plug is saying it's not.

    In this case, BotSpawn is saying bots can't attack you, so I guess you have Peacekeeper on and no weapon out.
    If that's correct and you pulled out a weapon, the notifications would stop.
