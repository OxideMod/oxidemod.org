  12. Steenamaroo

    I think we've covered reset and respawn.

    The suicide timer is for bots who don't ever respawn.
    That's /botspawn toplayer, and bots which come with an airdrop.

    Because they're a 'one-off' there's no need to have respawn options for them.
    The suicide option is there to eliminate the possibility of them building up and up in numbers over time.
