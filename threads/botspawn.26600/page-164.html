  14. KrisBendix

    danredlord said: ↑
    Привет, как сделать ботов в том месте где я хочу и можно ли это вообще сделать?
    Click to expand...
    You mean custom locations?

    Code:
    Custom Locations. (data file)
Chat Commands.
'/botspawn add customname' - Adds a custom profile at your location**
'/botspawn remove customname'
'/botspawn move existingname' - relocates a custom profile to your location.
'/botspawn list' (shows all custom location names)
'/botspawn toplayer playername profile - You need to have a custom profile first.
    Remember that they will not spawn inside buildings and caves. Only on surfaces visible from the sky.
