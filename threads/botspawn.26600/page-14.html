  18. Steenamaroo

    Hi,
    'Ignore_Animals" would need to be set to true if you want them to ignore animals.

    'Bot Radius', as per overview, is not yet implemented.
    It is hoped that, eventually, each bot's range of free-roaming can be limited.

    Right now they will eventually travel away from the monument, which is why we have a temporary 'Reset' option.
    That option will respawn all bots at their monument every 15 minutes.

    Radius 200 means bots will spawn somewhere, at random, within 200m of your monument.
