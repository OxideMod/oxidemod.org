  20. Steenamaroo

    Hi Thorkil,
    If you want them to have specific weapons, give them a kit with that weapon in the hotbar.
    Kits override all default clothes/weapons/items, so you will need to give your bot clothing too.

    If you don't want them to drop their primary weapon, there's an option to disable weapon-drop-on-death.

    Any other weapons in the bots hotbar are destroyed on death, but items in the main container and clothes bar remain lootable.

    Thank you.
