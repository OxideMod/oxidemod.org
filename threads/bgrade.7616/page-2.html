  11. Big Lou XVI

    enims said: ↑
    I never had to do anything before. Any admin just had access to it. What do I have to do to get it to work? Big Lou, what did you do to fix it?
    Click to expand...
    The permissions for the plugin work based on what permission group you are in, e.g. 'players' or 'admin'. I thought the mod worked off authlevel e.g. 0,1,2. I realised I was still in the 'player' permission group but had had authlevel 2. so by adding myself to the admin group i had to use "oxide.usergroup add <steamid> admin". This then made me part of the admin permission group, and the plugin then worked fine for me. It was my mistake that it did not work for me in the first place.
     
    Big Lou XVI, Jul 5, 2015
