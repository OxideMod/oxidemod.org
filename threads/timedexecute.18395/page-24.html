    I have noticed that TimedExecute will miss a command if server is lagging at the exact moment that it is supposed to trigger a command. I have actually doubled up the most important commands I use it for, for this reason.... setting a secondary duplicate a minute later in the event of lag during the first event.

    Also, you probably already know this, but everything in timed execute needs to be in order, so from 00:00:00 to 23:59:59. You can't put anything else after the end. And you also can't have anything out of order.... or else plugin will stop working until the next restart as soon as it reaches the out of order content.
