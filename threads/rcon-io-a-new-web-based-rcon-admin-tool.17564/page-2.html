    Thank you! As for now RCON:IO is created to make your browser do all the job. RCON:IO do not save any information about your Rust-server. It's no services connected to your Rust-server when you close your browser or tab. It will be possible to make timed chat messages in the future (but only when you have RCON:IO open). The main reason to this is security and trust issues. I would not like to be responsible for a leak of 1000+ RCON password if the database get compromised.
