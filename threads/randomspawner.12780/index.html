    LaserHydra submitted a new resource:

    Random Spawner - Randomly spawn a specific amount of an entity on the map

    Random Spawner allows you to randomly Spawn a specific amount of an entity on the map.

    Permissions:
    • rwarp.use
    • rwarp.getprefabs
    Chat Commands:
    • /rspawn <amount> "<entity prefab>"
    Console Commands:
    • rspawn <amount> "<entity prefab>"
    • getprefabs
