    Dora submitted a new resource:

    Blessing of The Gods - Players will be randomly blessed by a God

    Blessings:
    • God of Life - Recover max health to user.
    • God of Damnation - Take away 25% current HP from user.
    • God of Gluttony - Recover max water & food to user.
    • God of Poverty - Does nothing.
    • God of Death - Instant KO to user.
    • God of Plague - Poison the user. (est 20% hp)
    • God of War - Give 10% XP based on current unspent XP.
    Blessing Chances:
    • 40% - God of Poverty
