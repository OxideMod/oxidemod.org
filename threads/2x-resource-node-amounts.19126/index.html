    I guess it's actually a hack'n solution. You must config your server to double spawn points for nodes. It's on /Mods/Resources.defaults.cfg (wich I suppose you must rename to Resources.cfg to make the server/oxide actually load it)...

    In line 97 for example is where you determine the position of the stone nodes. Double it and it'll spawn there twice meaning you may have 500 resource at that point (2x nodes actually). At least that's the way I've seen implemented so far. There's also a "density" parameter... You should mess with it and see if there's any results.
