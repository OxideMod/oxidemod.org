    VehicleManager - Customize/install/remove/switch vehicle attachments

    Vehicle Manager for Hurtworld. Let's you customize your claimed vehicle by installing, removing or switching vehicle attachments (currently blocked by the game). You can now have your custom Roach or Goat : )

    You can click Download Now to get the plugin or Ask Questions / Get Support if you have any questions, on the right side of this web page. There is also a Support the Developer button if you enjoy the plugin...
