  9. Noviets

    I've submitted the plugin, it's awaiting approval.
    Basically it's a reporting plugin with additional chat logging capability, which you can either have it logging specific words or everything.
    The reports work like a mailbox. Once a report is submit you get announced (if you're online) or it will tell you when you connect how many unread reports you have. You can view unread reports with /unread to read them, and they will be automatically removed from the unread list.

    You can view a list of all reports and the chat log in a json file that's a lot easier to read with a timestamp and playername. The reports also include the SteamID's of the players for easy banning if they are not online at the time.

    The plugin is called Reporting

    It was approved: Reporting for Hurtworld | Oxide
    Enjoy
