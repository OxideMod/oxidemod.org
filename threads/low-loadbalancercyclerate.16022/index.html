  2. cowtrix

    loadbalancercyclerate represents how many times the LoadBalancer (a class that schedules work) gets through it's list of stuff to do per second.

    10-20 is perfectly fine, you should only be worried if it's less than 1. If it does get below 1 and you still have high,stable FPS, you can increase the LoadBalancer budget with `loadbalancerframebudget <value>`.
     
    cowtrix, Feb 23, 2016
