    BuildCost - Displays an approximate build cost for a structure and deployables

    BuildCost is a simple plugin meant to give an approximation of the amount of resources required to build a structure and its deployables.

    The cost of building block entities is determined by taking the cost of the current grade and adding the cost of the twigs grade. The cost does NOT take into account multiple upgrades! If you upgraded a foundation from twig to stone, then from stone to armored, the cost of that foundation will only include armored cost + twig cost.
