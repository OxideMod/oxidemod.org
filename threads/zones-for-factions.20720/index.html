Rust Zones for factions

Discussion in 'Plugin Requests' started by Shotgun Teddy, Aug 31, 2016.

  1. Shotgun Teddy

    I'd love to see this implemented somehow. I'd love to make Faction-based Cities/Towns and have zone permissions setup so only members of certain Factions could enter the town; a fun method involved in joining a faction would be great too, like talking to an NPC or purchasing a "faction pass" from a Vendor from Server Rewards, or having to meet an XP requirement to join a faction.

    Basically create create a Zone, assign that Zone to a group or faction and only give that group or faction permission to enter the Zone, or to build, deploy, loot, gather, etc. in that zone.

    Maybe make an option to allow non-faction-affiliated players to enter and what not, but not other factions/groups etc.

    Could do a lot of cool stuff with this!

    This could work with K1llYous Clans too! Anything that basically allows a player to join a group of other players, and then give that group ZoneManager permissions.

    On a sidenote* it'd be great to see ZoneManager allow you to set individual permissions per zone created.
    I.e. Zone ID 123 has X and Y permissions
    Zone ID 321 has W and Z permissions etc.
     
    Shotgun Teddy, Aug 31, 2016
