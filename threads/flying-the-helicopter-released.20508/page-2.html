  7. Colon Blow

    Ok, anyone wanna play with my Work in Progress Fun plugin to fly Heli, here it is

    use /flyheli or F1 command flyheli (recommend bind key) to start flying, and to stop and remove heli.
    Note 1 : you must be a admin and can use noclip .no permissions needed. (for now. working on it)
    Note 2 : player is visible (and can be damaged) while heli is flying. I use the vanish plugin to remove from view. (for now. working on it)

    Heli follows you around while nocliping. you have full control to shoot guns (left mouse), rockets (right mouse button) and napalm (middle mouse button).
    To stop just type the /flyheli or console command. the heli will be removed.
    If heli is shot down, it will release player and Heli AI will take over and fall to ground and explode as normal.

    I know the camera acts weird when back up and stuff. Just post any crazy bugs you find
    its for fun...
