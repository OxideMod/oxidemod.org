    In your server control panel:
    select: Mod: oxide
    select: Steam update: Yes, run update
    click green button: Update settings and restart server
    wait.
    when its done, stop your server.
    download last oxide version. uncompress on your computer all the files. Upload all the files with ftp (you will have error about csharpcompiler.exe, it's ok)
    start your server.

    It's how you can manually update oxide anytime you need
