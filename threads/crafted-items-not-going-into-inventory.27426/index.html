  5. ignignokt84

    It seems like you have a plugin that modifies your inventory slots for some reason or another - like giving you 30 slots to do some behind-the-scenes item management, even though you can only see 24. When players craft items, those items can be placed into the "extra" slots that you can't see, effectively making it seem like the items were lost. Unloading said plugin probably wouldn't instantly remove those extra slots unless the plugin was explicitly coded to do so, but on death the player's inventory would be moved to a new container with no extra slots, moving those hidden items into visible slots (which sounds like what your player experienced with the 3 beds).

    You may want to search your plugins and see if any contain references to containerMain.capacity. This might indicate the player's main inventory size is "altered" by that plugin.
