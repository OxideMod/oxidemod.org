    k1lly0u submitted a new resource:

    ServerRewards - Earn reward points for playtime and player referrals, then use those points to buy kits

    Earn rewards points for playtime and use those points to buy kits. Also earn points for referring players to your server. Setup kits with items for players to claim as rewards with individual costs, the user can then select what kit to purchase using the points they have earnt.

    Chat Commands
    /rewards claim <name> - Claims the reward kit
    /rewards check - Display current reward points and total time played on server
    /rewards list - Lists available rewards...
