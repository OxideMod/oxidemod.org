    Jinnai #low said: ↑
    some one have a icon bundle for this plugin?
    Click to expand...
    My advice ('cause it's what I do) is to pull from rust.wikia.com . Just find the item you want, and right click on the picture and "Copy Image Location" (in Firefox, your browser may vary). For example, if you want the Small Present, go to Small Present and get the link which is:
    Code:
    http://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/play-rust/images/d/da/Small_Present_icon.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/100?cb=20151217230749
    You can trim everything after the '.png' to get
    Code:
    http://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/play-rust/images/d/da/Small_Present_icon.png
    For custom images, I upload 'em to TinyPic and then send them over using the "LoadImages" command.

    Otherwise, you've already got 'em. Got to the application's folder and copy 'em out. On my Windows PC they're under:
    Code:
    D:\Program Files (x86)\SteamLibrary\steamapps\common\Rust\Bundles\items
    You might have 'em under the C:\ Drive depending on your setup. For Mac/Linux, your on your own. XD
