    Looks good, although you may want to remove everything after .png in the link for clearer images in /s.

    Once you are done editing the rewards.json file, do these 3 steps to get images to appear:
    1. reload ServerRewards
    2. in RCON type loadimages and wait for it to say [Info] [ServerRewards] Saved reward data (can take sometime depending on amount of images to load)
    3. reload ServerRewards once more
    Your images should now appear in /s.
