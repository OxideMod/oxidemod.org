    The hours are tracked by ConnectionDB, but the ranking system is done by PlayerRankings. So what must've happened was when you restarted your server, ConnectionDB went back a slight bit in time which could possibly be because it didn't have enough time to save, but PlayerRankings did not as they granted your rank and was "saved", but you still get both ranks as your level up is already there, and you go back to the original rank you were. This should fix itself once you reach 200 hours once more.
