    If its an issue with them stashing in containers you could do the noboxloot option from the plugin Zone Manager. It will prevent players from seeing and placing items inside any container within a zone. It may be an issue though if you are wanting players to be able to loot it after an event is over as you would have to turn the noboxloot off or remove/disable the zone after each event.
