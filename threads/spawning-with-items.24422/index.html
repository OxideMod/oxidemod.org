  3. Freaky

    As Resistance said: Kits for Rust | Oxide.

    1) Empty your inventory
    2) Add in your inventory the items that you want players to have (10 blueberries and 1 hatchet)
    3) /kit add "autokit"
    4) /kit authlevel 2 hide true
    5) /kit items

    For testing: F1--> kill

    P.S.: make the exact steps, don't rename the kit!
