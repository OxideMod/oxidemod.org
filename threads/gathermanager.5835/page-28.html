    Well, from what I can tell, you won't be gathering 30x the average amount of wood because you set 'tree' to the increase. You have to set the individual resources to a gather.rate, not a resource node. Etc: what you would want to type into the console is, "gather.rate dispenser 30", and this would make chopping wood, mining stone, and skinning animals 30 times as resourceful. Simply replacing 'tree' with 'wood' will do what you are trying to do. Hopefully I made some sense.
