    insane8472 submitted a new resource:

    Door Share - Allows players to share all doors they own with other players.

    Introduction
    I would like to thank, unbeknownst to them, Reneb for his Prod (who owns), which helped me learn what I needed to accomplish this plugin.

    Description
    Allows players to share all doors they own with other players.

    Permissions
    This plugin uses oxide permissions.

    canShareDoors - Allows players to share their doors with other players
    canCheckOwner - Allow players to check the owner of doors
    Code:
    oxide.grant <group|user> <name|id> canShareDoors
