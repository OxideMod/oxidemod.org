  16. Canopy Sheep

    When Facepunch gives an item durability, that item can no longer be stacked above one regardless of what you do. The game overlooks the variable, if it didn't, you would have an easy exploit to instantly repair items by stacking them together. The check for the plugin itself is simply there to prevent any confusion on why items with durability aren't stacking above one.
