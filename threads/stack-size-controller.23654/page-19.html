  5. PryMary

    Riddle said: ↑
    Is there a way to get exactly 2X stacks of Vanilla with a command or something instead of editing everything? Or does anyone have a 2X stacksize config?
    Click to expand...
    From the overview:

    Commands
    • /stack item.short.name stacksize (EX: /stack ammo.rocket.hv 64)
    • /stackall 65000 (Sets the stack size to 65000) <------------------------ That would be the command you need
    • Console command "stack" and "stackall" also work same way as the above.
