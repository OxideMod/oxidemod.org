  8. Deicide666ra

    LimeBench is configurable you can increase both the overall craft rate and the rate when you are in range of a cupboard.

    There's a good chance my mod would interfere with other crafting speed mods because I'm changing the craft speed of blueprints straight into the game definitions on the fly for each new task and NOT resetting them until another item of the same type begins crafting.

    I'm also toying with the craft queue for each new item craft, so again, this might not work well with other mods trying to do the same.

    That said, why use both? Limebench does both cupboard boost and base boost, should cover all your needs. If you need to mod the speed of some items in a different manner, use ItemConfig and change the base time in there.
