    Use "Server Logs" Plugin it will make folders under 'scriptfiles/logs' you will see these
    - Cubes (Placement, Damage, Destruction)
    - Entities (Damage, Destruction)
    - Chat (Global, Guilds)
    - Events (Connections, disconnections of players)
    - Optional: Special (If enabled, damage to crests and sleepers will be logged seperately.)
