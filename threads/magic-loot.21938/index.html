    Norn submitted a new resource:

    Magic Loot - Simple loot system

    Simple loot system without large loot tables and everything else commonly associated with loot plugins.

    How it works:
    Amount of components/items in Loot Containers go by vanilla rates x multiplier. So say in vanilla there are 2 springs in a box and you have Magic Loot's multiplier set at 2, 4 springs will be in the box.
