  8. sqroot

    This issue is caused by a particular modified version of RemoverTool that allows you to remove cupboards (Maybe the version I made, maybe another one).
    Basically, the raycast may sometimes consider the cupboard radius as the cupboard itself, because it's the same entity type.
    To fix this, stick with the version that doesn't allow you to remove cupboards instead.
    You've essentially got the choice between players having to be careful when removing and players having to smash cupboards to remove them.
