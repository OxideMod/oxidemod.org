    Day Vote - Allows player voting to skip night cycles.

    Introduction
    This is my own take on skipping night cycles. Includes several new options and configurations.

    Description
    Allows player voting to skip night cycles.

    Notes:
    • AllowVoteChange - If true, players may change there vote as many times as they wish.
    • AllowVoteNights - How often a day vote is called. For example, 1 = every night, 2 = every other night and so on.
