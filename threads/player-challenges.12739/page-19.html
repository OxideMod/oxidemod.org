    iYRe said: ↑
    is there a way to see who is leading various things other than the GUI, like a txt file?
    Click to expand...
    That information can be found in \oxide\data\challenge_data.json. It's a json encoded file (easy to decode in PHP and parse into an array, use print_r to print it in an 'ok' format). You could do anything with that file, like downloading it onto your webserver and displaying it with a nice markup (example: Team Howl - Portal)
