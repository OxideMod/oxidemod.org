  1. Mortu88

    Mortu88 submitted a new resource:

    RemoveClaim - Remove the claim of the car you're driving

    When sitting in a roach or goat, type '/removeclaim <ownerName>' to remove the claim from a car. You can find <ownerName> through the interface of the vehicle.

    Chat Commands
    • /removeclaim <ownerName> Remove the claim from the vehicle claimed by <ownerName> if the player is the driver or passenger of this vehicle.
    Warning: this mod might remove the wrong claim if there are players using the same name.

    Configuration file:
    Code:
