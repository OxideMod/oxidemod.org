  9. NiueanTurtle

    1. Establish which command you want to bind to a key.
    It needs to be a command that ordinarily works in the CONSOLE, like "env.kill" or "env.time 800", etc.

    2. You cannot bind a CHAT command, examples are "/info" or "/remove"

    3. Go to the CONSOLE and type "bind [key] [command]"
    Example: "bind f noclip" and this will bind the letter f to let you fly, without having to type noclip in the console.

    Ignore the quotation marks.
     
    NiueanTurtle, Jan 30, 2018
