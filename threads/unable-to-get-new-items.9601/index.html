  3. Wulf Community Admin

    Oxide doesn't do anything with your inventory unless you have a plugin that does blocks those commands or restricts it.

    Keep in mind that "owner" and RCON are unrelated. RCON is the remote protocol used with tools such as Rusty and has no auth level, whereas the in-game F1 console would require owner or moderator permission (auth level 2 or 1) to use most Rust commands.

    I have tested the F1 item menu though, and it is working perfectly fine, so it's likely you are blocking it somehow or do not have permission.
