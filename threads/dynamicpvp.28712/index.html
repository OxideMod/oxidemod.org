  1. CatMeat

    CatMeat submitted a new resource:

    DynamicPVP - Creates temporary PVP zones around SupplyDrops for use with TruePVE and ZoneManager

    Bring a little PVP action to your PVE server!!!

    Creates temporary PVP zones around supply drops (requires TruePVE and ZoneManager).

    As soon as a supply drop (not the Cargo Plane) has spawned, a PVP zone is created with a default radius of 100.
    The PVP zone has a 10 minute (600 seconds) duration by default and will be deleted at the end of the duration.
    Both radius and duration can be customized in the config file.
