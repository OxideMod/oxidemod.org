  1. Wulf Community Admin

    DaBludger submitted a new resource:

    Scuba Steve - Be the SEAL, this will protect you from drowning and cold damage while swimming

    Give your players a reason to use the Hazmat cloths! This will allow the players to swim under water and take less damage from drowning and cold. So the players will have another way of getting out to the ice bergs or building bridges with out the fear of drowning to death halfway.

    This has a config file that you can assign the % of damage deduction per peace of hazmat cloths. If the numbers add up to or above 1.0 then the players will take no damage.
