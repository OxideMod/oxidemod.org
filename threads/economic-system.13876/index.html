  10. HanS0l0

    Hey there,

    i was thinking about a plugin that provides a basic economy system that creates an ingame currency , lets call them tokens for the moment. nothing fancy but it should have some key features

    Have 2 Safe locations on the Map -Trader Town- and -Scrapyard-
    They should be a no building area , and all weapons disabled
    ( i dont know how limited the options are right now, but is it even possible to create such things with these kind of restrictions ? )

    At the Trader Town , there should be some kind of npc(?) or any kind of container that can take in players resources and recycle them and reward them with tokens

    At the Scrapyard its basically the same as the trader town except it would need some kind of mechanic to reward the player with specific items they chose from like a list and pay with their tokens.


    The other approach i was thinking about would be to take the materials directly from the players inventory and reward them instantly, like you would need 500 amber to get a powerful engine - this would be easier to program but would take away the "content/adventure" side of things when you acutally have to go places with the resources you farmed to generate tokens and furthermore when trading in those tokens for better items.
    Additionally the system could have a system where players can set bounties on someone's head , and the killer would receive the bounty. And simple transfer system between players like /pay player amount X.
    TL;DR
    eco system that creates Tokens ; players turn in resources get rewarded tokens; can buy better items with tokens

    thanks for reading,
    i know this is a rather big project, and this is just the basic idea with still lots to figure out, but i feel like it would be very well received by the playerbase

    greets, Han
