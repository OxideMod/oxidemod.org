  3. Willman42

    @RussaX - High External wall is already on the list of items that will decay, as specified in the TwingsDecay.json at the top section called "blocks." And there actually is a way to specify tier-only decays. In your TwigsDecay.json file, under the "damage" section there are values you can put for each building grade, as well as Barricades, Gates, and Ladders. If you don't want a tier to decay at all, specify 0 as the value.
