    I will add option to disable skills and their UIs later.

    XP loss is based on your current level, if you are level 10 and have 80% exp, and you should loose 60% exp on death, that means you'll be level 10 and 20% experience.
    Every hour spent alive, you'll loss less experience when killed. For example if you set XPPercentToLoose to 100%, after 5 hours you'll only loose 50% instead of 100%, hours counts even when you are offline. There is also 10mins grace period, that when you die within first 10mins you don't loose any experience.
