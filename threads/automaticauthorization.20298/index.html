    k1lly0u submitted a new resource:

    AutomaticAuthorization - Add your friends list or clan mates to a cupboard or turret auth list using 1 command

    Requires either Clans or FriendsAPI

    Add all your clan mates or friends from your friend list to any tool cupboard or auto turret authorization list with 1 chat command. You must own the entity!

    Chat Commands
    /auth clan - Add your clans mates to the auth list
    /auth friends - Add your friends to the auth list

    Permissions
    automaticauthorization.use - Required to use
