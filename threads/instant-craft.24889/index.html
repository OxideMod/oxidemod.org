    Instant Craft - Makes crafting of items instant and prevents items being dropped after

    This plugin is petty self-explaining. It makes craft of every item in-game absolutely instant.

    Include blacklist (so item can't be crafted at all) and normal speed list(so the item will be crafted with the default speed)

    Also it prevents items from being droped after craft is finished - it checks for free space in the player inventory and will allow to craft only if there is a space available.
