    Is this sooo hard to find on that side????
    Code:
    How can i simply speed up the oven types?


OvenMultipliers does control the tick time for each cook processing of the available oven types.

By default they are all at 1.0, which is 0.5 seconds per tick.
Raising this value to 2 results in a lowered tick of 0,25 seconds per each cook processing.
This is limited actually to a max of 10.0 to prevent possible performance impacts.

The OvenMultipliers are in result mainly meant for those people who simply want to increase the cooking times by a fast and easy way.
They are not designed to run 100x or 1000x increased speed rates.
