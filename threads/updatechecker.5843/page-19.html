    Its pretty funny. I added those warnings so you know when there is something wrong with the plugins support for the Updater and everyone thinks this plugin is broken. Before the plugin would just not mention those.

    The few plugins which Updater wasn't able to check for, are most likely all unmaintained or have an invalid resourceID set.

    The plugins mentioned to have no ResourceID set, literally have no ResourceID set which is required to check for updates.

    So what can you do to get rid of the warnings?

    You can not do much about the unmaintained ones unless you get somebody to continue them. If they are maintained, tell the author to fix their resource ID.

    For the ones who entirely miss the resourceID, you just tell the author to add the resourceID for supporting Updater.
