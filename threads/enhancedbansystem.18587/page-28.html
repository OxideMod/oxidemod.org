  2. Slut

    In your config you have the choice of 5 different ban systems (different ways the bans are stored). Foreach ban system that you have enabled in the config you are able to do /banlist (BanSystem). If you have native enabled (writes bans to bans.cfg which is the vanilla ban system) then you would do /banlist native.
