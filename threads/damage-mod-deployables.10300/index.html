  1. Colon Blow

    Colon Blow submitted a new resource:

    Damage Mod : Deployables - Prevents Damage to Numerous Deployables

    Geared toward PVE servers mostly. This plugin will block all damage to most deployable items.

    Works well with my other plugins :
    Damage Mod : Buildings
    Damage Mod : Players

    Items include:

    Tool Cupboard's
    Signs
    Sleeping Bags
    Mining Quarries
    Water Catchers
    Furnaces
    Lanterns
    Camp Fires
    Research Tables
    Repair Benches

    Did not see the need to have all damage types listed for all items individually. So this plugin just blocks damage by default.

    More info coming soon.
