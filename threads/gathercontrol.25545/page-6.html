    Click to expand...
    HQM in Rust is issued as a bonus at the end of mining of metal ore. The plugin multiplies the number of HQMs by the multiplier that you set in the config. With such a large multiplier, as you do, there may be problems with obtaining resources due to the size limitations of the stack.
