    Click to expand...
    In GatherControl you choose the time of the beginning of night and the beginning of day gathering rating in the parameters in-game time. TimeOfDay changes the duration of the day and night in real time and does not change the in-game time, that is, it serves to accelerate or slow down the in-game time. So I do not see any problems for using plugins together.
