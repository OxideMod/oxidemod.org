    Scorpyon submitted a new resource:

    Guild Info - Know thy Enemy! Find out how many members a guild has and which of them are online!

    This little plugin will give you a quick heads up about a player's guild; how many members his guild has, who they are and whether they are currently online.

    View attachment 9638

    Players will need to have logged on at least once to be included in the guild census. It will allow you to check offline players if they have been added to the guild info list.

    Use /guildinfo <player_name> to see information about that player's guild.
