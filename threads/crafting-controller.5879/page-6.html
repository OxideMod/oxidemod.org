    Click to expand...
    Oxide 2 is for the default (former Experimental) servers, Oxide 1.18 and prior is for the legacy servers. If I recall correctly you don't need a plugin to reduce the crafting times and can just use a console command crafting.timescale 0.5 for half-craft, 1 is normal, crafting.instant true for instant crafting for everyone. And if you want to block specific items from being crafted you could use this plugin: http://oxidemod.org/resources/crafting-controller.6/
