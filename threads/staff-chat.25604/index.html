    Twistedwheel submitted a new resource:

    Staff Chat - Private chat for staff members and other VIP players

    Massive credit to @Wulf for his help and patience, and for converting the plugin from Rust to Universal!

    This plugin adds three configurable chat groups that allow admins, moderators and donators to speak to each other privately.

    This plugin uses Oxide's permission system. To assign a permission, use grant user <name or steam id> <permission>. To remove a permission, use revoke user <name or steam id> <permission>.
