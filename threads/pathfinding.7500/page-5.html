  4. Mr.SnivelUMPagus™

    markbillybob said: ↑
    any idea how to fix the Reached max depth error?
    Click to expand...
    You can't. It only warns you when you hurt an NPC from a location that the NPC can't find a path to you. Like when you are in a building, or on a high roof. I think max depth is limited to 1000, so to fix you need to be on a level in the range where it can find a path to you. If you hurt an NPC and it does not move when you have defend set to true, you will get the warning.
    I tried to set max depth to zero, and it gets the error even when you are on a rock only a little higher than the NPC. When I tried to set max depth to 10000, it seemed like it wasn't any better than 1000.
    It also gets the warning when you shoot an NPC from a window, or on a roof if you are not on the edge of the roof. If you are on the edge of a roof, the NPC will come after you and you won't get the warning as long as the roof isn't too high.

    So since the range of weapons and how far NPC's render is farther than the max depth, we will always get the warning.
