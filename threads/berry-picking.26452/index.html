    Hello,

    I don't know if something like this has been pitched before, and frankly I would be surprised if it hasn't, however, figured I'd give it a shot anyway.

    So, the idea is that there are bushes all over the maps of the Rust Islands. Would it be possible for someone to make a mod where a player walks up to a bush, presses a key, and it searches that bush for berries. With a success percentage of getting blackberries or blueberries and (maybe, a stretch) a cooldown on the bush before it can be picked again.

    I feel these items are underused. My server, for example, has a higher level of metabolism. so, players are constantly trying to obtain food. Berries are good for thirst, which is harder to keep up. Just curious if anyone has ideas or if another mod took care of this. I mean, I compared it to the fishing mod. You use a spear or crossbow to hit water and you'd obtain a fish on a success percentage. I'd imagine the idea is the same. But, I don't know much at all about coding, so... I could be way off base here. Just an idea I figure I'd throw out there.

    If the idea of the fishing mod could be reworked for berry picking essentially, is what I am getting at. If an item is needed to collect berries, perhaps something like the bucket or the small wooden crate (as if it was a crate/pint of berries). Just ideas to see if anything sparks inspiration.

    Thanks for your time in reading this!
