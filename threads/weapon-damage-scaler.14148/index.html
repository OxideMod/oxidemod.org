    Shady757 submitted a new resource:

    Weapon Damage Scaler - Allows scaling of player damage per weapon/ammo

    Weapon Damage Scaler is a plugin that allows you to scale a player's damage per weapon, and/or ammo type.

    The plugin itself is pretty simple, here's how to scale damages:
    Example of console command usage: setscale "Assault Rifle" "0.923". This will scale the damage of the Assault Rifle to 0.923.

    Chat command works the same way: /setscale <weaponnname> <x.x>
    Setting ammo is just as easy: /setscale "HV 5.56 Rifle Ammo" 1.2

  6. Shady757

    Shady757 updated Weapon Damage Scaler with a new update entry:

    1.0.3

    Added preliminary support for Explosives:
    To scale explosives damage is like everything else, just type setscale "Timed Explosive Charge" 1.1 -- or whatever value you want. Keep in mind the scaler for Explosives is much more sensitive than weapons/other ammo.

    For rockets, simply type the rocket ammo type, e.g, setscale "Rocket" 1.0, setscale "High Velocity Rocket" 1.2, etc.

    Keep in mind this setting ignores the "PlayersOnly" option.
