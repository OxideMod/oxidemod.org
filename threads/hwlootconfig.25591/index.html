    After looking at the config file, it will allow you to raise/lower the gather rates and chances of getting additional loot like bark and amber from logs.

    For example:
    "StackSize": 4,
    "RandomVariance": 6

    Means the base amount you'll get is 4, then a chance of getting 1-6 more based off rng.
