  7. Steenamaroo

    RuArt said: ↑
    Recent (Recently active number)
    How many seconds ago did the player come in?
    Click to expand...
    Sort of.
    A number is assigned to every player who joins.

    If you're sorting an online table with online players at the top, an additional sort (by recent) will allow your offline players to be in order of most recently joined.
    You'd only ever use it if you're sorting your data on a website.


    From the update notes.

    SQL order by status then order by recent ==
    • player 1 - online - (5)
    • player 2 - online - (2)
    • player 3 - offline - (4)
    • player 4 - offline -(3)
    • player 5 - offline - (1)
    etc
