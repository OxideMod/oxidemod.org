  10. Gliktch

    toeknee said: ↑
    If all I wanted to do was have explosive charges drop on every airdrop how would I configure that?
    Click to expand...
    Delete or rename your lootspawnlists txt file, then reboot your server (or oxmin.reload lootspawnlists in console) to generate a default drop list. Then, edit the new lootspawnlists txt and add to SupplyDropSpawnListMaster, an Explosive Charges entry with weight 999, while setting the min/max for the master list to 2/2, and nodupes true.

    Technically that should work (so each time an airdrop happens it'll pick Explosive Charges AND one of SupplyDropA, SupplyDropB, etc), though I haven't tested it, so give yourself a dozen supply signals on server reboot and make sure you test it before letting your players loose on the drops I'd suggest going over the mountains and dropping them miles away from everything if you have a populated server (the Portal Chatgun mod is excellent for this, teleport to any point you can see).
