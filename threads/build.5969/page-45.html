  19. Venomous

    There are players complaining that they are 100% privilege to build anywhere on the map. I had a complaint prior to this to ban them, went to them, and they explained that they didn't realize it was some glitch in Rust currently. 6/4/2016 I deleted them with the Builder plugin, using /erase. After relogging, their "ability" was gone. This was currently the only way to stop it. This issue has been intermittent with what players are targeted, and has been recurring many times.

    Not sure if this is an on-going issue, after scrounging I saw that most players in the past - especially admin, had an issue with being building blocked everywhere. This was the opposite, and it's scary because it was three regular players. I assure you, they were not hacking. I was informed by FacePunch Studios to let some of the plugin designers that affect building privileges know of this issue, hopefully someone has this problem as well, or the developers can help, thank you!
