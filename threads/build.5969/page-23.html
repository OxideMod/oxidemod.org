    Hey, had an "awesome" idea tonight, it can be so simple...
    If you want to add a foundation to a triangle foundation, do it that way:
    - on the side you want to add a foundation, build 2 pillars.
    - use this pillars to get a regular floor on top of the triangle foundation
    - (weird to describe, lets say you have a triangle foundation with a square floor as a roof)
    - if you got problems placing the roof on two pillars only, build a wall first and then the floor
    - once you have a floor above the triangle foundation, use "/build foundation -3" and do this on the floor on the side you want to add the foundation to the triangle foundation one floor below.
    - tadaaa you just added a foundation perfectly to a triangle foundation...

    I know this sounds weird, once you got the steps its a matter of seconds
