    Trentu submitted a new resource:

    Clear Inventory - Clear the inventory of any player

    Clear the inventory of any player.

    Commands:
    - /clear | Clears your inventory
    - /clear <name> | Clears the inventory of other players ("/clear Derpy")

    Permission:
    - cinventory.clear | used for /clear
    - cinventory.clearother | user for /clear <name>
