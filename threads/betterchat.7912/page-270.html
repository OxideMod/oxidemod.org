  19. UnicornWeed

    Quantum said: ↑
    About the BetterChat Group Manager. Really nice work btw. Whats the priority setting?
    Click to expand...
    Priority is a number given to each group (0-infinity) with the zero group being the most important. This means when a user is in more than 1 group (admin=0 + default=1) but you only have the setting "Maximal Titles": 1, his admin title will only be shown. If he's in 4 different groups but you only allow 2 titles, the first 2 most important group titles will be shown only. Hopefully this answered your question
