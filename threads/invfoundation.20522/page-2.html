  14. sami37

    Dubz said: ↑
    Hi Sami,

    If I use:
    "UseBuildingOwner": false,
    "UseEntityOwner": false

    Will this mean any foundation that is covered by a TC be protected?
    Click to expand...
    When you enable BuildingOwner and/or EntityOwner, it will check if the attacker own the building/entity and if he own it, the player will be able to damage the building/entity.
    If you disable it with false, it will just ignore this check from BuildingOwner and/or EntityOwner plugins.
    Anyway, if you are the builder of the foundation, you will be able to damage it with or without BuildingOwner and EntityOwner plugins
     
    sami37, Jan 15, 2017
