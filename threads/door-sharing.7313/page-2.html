  6. Deicide666ra

    The doorshare plugin in legacy made it so that when you share with your buddy (/share buddyname) ALL doors that you owned would be openable by the buddy, whatever the code is, without even having to enter it. It's a convenience thing more than anything... When you have a clan you just /share with all clan members and if you need to kick one out, you just /unshare and he instantly looses access to all doors.

    There was another plugin similar to it that controlled whether or not you could use a sleeping bag based on base ownership. If you didn't have access you would be instakilled when you spawned. This was to prevent raiders from putting sleeping bags across your walls (a classic exploit back then) or use sleeping bags from inside your base to make multiple runs.
