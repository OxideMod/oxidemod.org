    Mordeus submitted a new resource:

    day7 - Allows players to check when the next horde night is.

    A simple plugin that allows players to check when the next horde night is. The message is displayed as Days, Hours, Minutes in chat in green at first, then yellow within 5 days, then red within 3 days.

    Chat command: /day7

    Credits:
    Based in the mod by Geesu called seven days helper, looking at his code gave me ideas on how to make it work.
    I also took a look at OpenFunRus's code, which was very helpful in getting things working right in the chat...
