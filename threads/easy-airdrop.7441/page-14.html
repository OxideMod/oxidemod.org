  13. OneBigMillipede

    BlueGiGames said: ↑
    When I add it to my server, and I do /airdrop it says "You don't have permission to use this command" even though I made myself owner when making the server. Please Help.
    Click to expand...
    Console Commands:
    • airdrop - Calls airdrop to random location
    • airdrop player <player> - Calls airdrop to player
    • airdrop pos <x> <z> - Calls airdrop to position
    • massdrop <count> - Calls multiple airdrops to random locations

    Permissions:
    • airdrop.call - needed for /airdrop
    • airdrop.call.player - needed for /airdrop player
    • airdrop.call.position - needed for /airdrop pos
    • airdrop.call.mass - needed for /massdrop
    You need to type in the console/rusty (Rcon) grant user (your name) and the permission you want,
    for me it would be grant user onebigmillipede airdrop.call
     
    OneBigMillipede, Jun 25, 2016
