    Click to expand...
    There's not a way to do this currently. It also might be noted that there is not a clear "governing" ruleset for a player at any particular moment - the rulesets are evaluated based on both the attacker's location and the target's location. So a player standing in one spot could technically have overlapping rulesets depending on what they are trying to attack. The rulesets are also only evaluated when an entity is damaged, so there is no direct or saved association between a player and a ruleset.
