    @Bill Murray 2 - To elaborate a bit, the RuleSets are basically compressed versions of the "containers" from the previous version. The "links" that used to exist were the "rules" that are there now. So whereas before you had some container named "players" with a link to "players": false, now you have an Entity Group named "players", and you write a rule "players cannot hurt players"... It's similar functionally, but less complex overall.
