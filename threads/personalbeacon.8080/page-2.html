  10. VVoid

    Code:
    player.SetPlayerFlag(BasePlayer.PlayerFlags.IsDeveloper, true);
    I really wouldn't recommend using this workaround, however it's the only one way.
    Between developer on and off, the player has a small chance to use a several client-sided functions such as debug cam and even noclip (in this case they'll be instantly autobanned by server).

    Just be aware.
     
    VVoid, Apr 30, 2017
