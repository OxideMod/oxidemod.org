    Your config file is strongly corrupted.
    I strongly recommend a clean reinstall of my plugin.
    1. unload the plugin: oxide.unload OilCrate
    2. delete config and the plugin itself: OilCrate.json, OilCrate.cs
    3. download the latest version: out of the overview
    4. copy the new OilCrate.cs in your oxide/plugins folder
    5. do your settings in the config
    6. reload the plugin: oxide.reload OilCrate
    7. [optional] if you already have pump jacks on your server, run the command: oilcrate regenerate
    This should fix your problems with this plugin.
