    Joker :D said: ↑
    how to increase production by 3 times, for example, of 10 fuel and get 10-12 of oil?
    Click to expand...
    To get 10-12 crude oil out of 10 low grade fuel you have to set your config as follows.
    Code:
      "3. Oil gather rate (lower is faster)": {
    "1. Min (0.0 to ~200.0)": 8.0,
    "2. Max (0.0 to ~200.0)": 11.0
  }
    I will create an a list and post it soon in the overview.
     
    Kaleidos, Mar 3, 2017
