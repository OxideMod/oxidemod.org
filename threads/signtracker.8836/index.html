  1. Wulf Community Admin

    lederp ツ submitted a new resource:

    Sign Tracker - Track who edits signs on your server, provides name and steam ID.

    A few people have requested a way to track those who edit signs on their server.

    A few other plugins allow you to see who placed the sign, but not the last person to edit it. I have created this plugin for those who are on a community server and want to ensure those drawing things they shouldn't can be dealt with.

    No config as of yet.
