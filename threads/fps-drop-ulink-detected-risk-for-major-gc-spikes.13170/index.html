  9. Speno

    Those warnings mean there is too many RPCs being sent inside a single update loop frame.

    uLink detected risk for major GC spikes. Unreliable outgoing BitStreamPool (isTypesafe: True) overflow, pool capacity 1000, usage was 1721
    Frame lease stream pool exceeded by 20

    Both are different mechanisms that do essentially the same thing, a pool of bitstream objects that gets auto released at the end of the frame once the outgoing packets are sent.

    This is usually caused by something blocking the update loop for a second or two, then the game tries to catchup on things sending the backlog of rpcs out in a single frame.
     
    Speno, Dec 9, 2015
