    Click to expand...
    Limits work in a similar fashion you already want, if you set a player to have a limit permission that grants them 3, it will reset when the server time switches over to the next day. This means you can grant a cooldown of 10 seconds or however long and they can use those 3, but won't be able to after those 3 until the next day (server time).

    This may be expanded on/improved in the future, but this is how it works for now.
