    Click to expand...
    You need to grant the group you are in the following permissions
    oxide.grant group admin helicontrol.callheli
    Permissions are as follows:
    helicontrol.callheli (gives you access /callheli)
    helicontrol.killheli (gives you access /killheli)
    helicontrol.killgibs (gives you access to /killgibs)
    helicontrol.killfireballs (gives you access to /killfireballs)
    helicontrol.strafe (gives you access to /strafe)
    helicontrol.update (gives you access to /updatehelis)
    helicontrol.destination (gives you access to /helidest)
    helicontrol.admin (gives you permission to everything)

    Click to expand...
    Unknown command, for what? If you're granting the permission, using your console or RCON, you type, oxide.grant group <groupname> <permissonname>

    In this case, oxide.grant group admin helicontrol.admin.

    Then you can use any of HeliControl's commands, for example, /killheli, /callheli, etc. If you wish to grant the permission to a user instead of a group, replace group with user, and <groupname> with the user's name.
