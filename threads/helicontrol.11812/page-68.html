    Click to expand...
    Strafe works just like the Helicopter usually does when it fires a rocket. It forcefully sets the target as that player, and will attempt to get into a strafing position and fire at where they were when it was told to strafe. If you force a strafe too far away from a player, the heli will sometimes lose the target after a few seconds. It will also stick around just like the heli normally would.
