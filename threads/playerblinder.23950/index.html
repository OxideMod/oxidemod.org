    redBDGR submitted a new resource:

    PlayerBlinder - Give an extra way of punishing players

    A new way of punishing players! This plugin give a range of different customisation to blinding players, including fill colours, images and text!

    Permissions:
    • playerblinder.admin
    Chat Commands:
    • /blind <playername> <length> "text" (also works as /blind <playername/id>)
    • /unblind <playername/id>
    Console Commands:
    • blind <playername/id> <length> "text" (also works as blind <playername>)
    • unblind <playername/id>
