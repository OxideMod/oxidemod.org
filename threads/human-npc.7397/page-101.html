    In oxide/data/ there is a HumanNPC.json file, inside of it is a list of every NPC you have active, under their names are a group of variables, one of which looks like this: "XP": 0.0,.

    i am just telling you: HumanNPC is one of the greatest and hardest Plugins at the same time. It gives you many possibilities to make awesome stuff happen, just as well as screwing everything up terribly..
    if you dont understand how to "add a weapon" to a npc, you will most probably screw up a lot of things. so listen to me or not, but this advice would have helped me when i started and i knew how to add hidden kits with authlevel 2, before i started with the npcs, so it was easy for me to understand, that i just have to give this kit to the npc while editing it. you dont seem to read overviews completely, thats why people might become a bit impatient with your questions.. even me

    Thats how i create Kits for my npc:
    Starting with Chatcommands:
    1. /kit add npctest
    2./kit hide true
    3./kit authlevel 2 (alt. authlevel 1 or npconly)
    4. after having all items in the right places of my inventory and holding the weapon in my hand,
    i type /kit items
    -> now the kit "npctest" is created & unavailable for players.
    5. looking at the npc i want to have the kit i type: /npc_edit
    6. /npc kit npctest
    -> now the npc has the kit npctest as spawn kit.
    7. /npc_end to stop editing npc (optional)
