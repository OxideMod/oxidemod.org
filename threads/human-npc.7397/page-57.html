    /kit add "Test" - The kit will be added and the name will be, Test
    /kit npconly true - Players cant use this kit.
    /kit hide true - Players cant see the kit in /kit list
    /kit authlevel 2 - Can use it with the corrrect authlevel
    /kit items - This will copy the items(Clothing, Weapons etc) you currently have in your inventory.

    When the kit is done, walk infront of your NPC and type:
    /npc_edit
    then
    /npc kit "Test"
