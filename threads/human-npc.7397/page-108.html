  6. An Innocent Cat

    Once you have the Kit plugin installed and it is loading properly, follow these steps:

    1. Join your server
    2. Empty your inventory, then give yourself (or craft) the items you want in the kit. Place the weapon you want your NPC to wield in your belt, and put on the clothes that you want your NPC to wear.
    3. Once you are happy with it, come up with a name for your kit and type this into chat: /kit add TheNameYouChose
    4. Press <ENTER>
    5. -VERY IMPORTANT- Type this into chat: /kit items
    STEP 5 is what actually saves the items in your current inventory to the kit that you created with the /kit add command. If you don't do step 5 then your kit will be created but it will be empty and you will suffer much annoyance. <---- voice of experience....

    Now you have a kit called whatever you entered in for TheNameYouChose. When you get HumanNPC loaded and running properly, follow the instructions in the overview to add an NPC. In the DATA folder of oxide on your server you should see HumanNPC.json. When you edit it, you will find an entry for the NPC you created, along with a big list of variables that are associated with that NPC. One of those variables looks like:

    "spawnkit": null,

    Replace null with whatever you chose for "TheNameYouChose" (keeping the quotes just in case, of course)

    Now when that NPC respawns, or you restart your server (or when you RCON execute oxide.reload HumanNPC), he or she should be carrying the weapon that you placed in the belt of that kit, and wearing the clothes that you equipped, and be carrying any other gear that you added to your inventory before you entered the /kit items command.

    It's a bit clunky, but you have to repeat this process for other NPCs if you want them to be carrying different stuff. Go back to your server, restart at STEP 2 and give the new kit a different name. Then when you create your new NPC, edit their entry in the json file and put then name of the new kit as their spawnkit variable, etc., etc., etc.

    That is all. Good luck and Happy Rusting.
