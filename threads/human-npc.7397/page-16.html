  4. deeplogic

    For anyone that wants their NPC's to be, if nothing else, not asleep until Reneb releases the patch, you can make this modification to the HumanNPC.CS file:

    Line 882:
    player.SendNetworkUpdate(BasePlayer.NetworkQueue.Positional);

    Modification:
    //player.SendNetworkUpdate(BasePlayer.NetworkQueue.Positional);

    The line will now be read as a comment and the error won't be produced. This will bring them out of their sleep but they'll have lost their ability to find you when attacking and won't move. I had a lot of NPC's that were stationed at key areas just to deliver messages. I couldn't have them sleeping on the job. If you're aren't in a similar situation, I would recommend just waiting for Reneb's update.
     
    deeplogic, May 27, 2015
