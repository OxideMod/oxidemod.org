  1. Jonty

    Jonty submitted a new resource:

    Political Survival - Become the President, tax the server, and subjugate everyone!

    Political Survival is a take on the Reign of Kings throne system. I found that style of game play very enjoyable and I'm sure at least one other person did too.

    In this plugin, you can become the server President, tax your subjects up to 25% of all resources gathered with a tool (so this means all Ores, Wood, Animal fat, meat, etc) and select a chest for it to go into automatically.

    If you die, you lose your presidency. If you are killed by another player, you lose it, and they succeed...
