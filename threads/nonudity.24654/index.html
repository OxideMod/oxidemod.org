    Absolut submitted a new resource:

    NoNudity - Gives pants and forces players to wear pants. For those that don't like lower body nudity

    This plugin gives players a pair of pants and then forces the player to keep wearing pants. The idea is to eliminate the "lower body" nudity some players don't care for.

    The config allows the admin to set a "pants" type and skin for the default "lower body" attire. This type can be shorts, a skirt, whatever.

    This does not prevent players from changing pants to short or a hazmat suit but rather just ensures that the lower body is always covered.
