    Yes, in the upper part you have the items that are available, below you just put the "stores".
    And yes - you can add new things
    In the config you will finde this:
    Code:
    "Apple": {
      "buy": "1",
      "img": "http://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/play-rust/images/d/dc/Apple_icon.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/100?cb=20150405103640",
      "item": "apple",
      "sell": "1"
    If you want to add something just keep it that way and do it like this:
    Code:
    "Hemp seed": {
      "buy": "100",
      "img": "http://vignette1.wikia.nocookie.net/play-rust/images/1/1c/Hemp_Seed_icon.png/revision/latest?cb=20160511082423",
      "item": "hemp seed",
      "sell": "90"
    "Hemp" ( you can name this however you want it to be named, if you want to make shops that sell them at different prices, just copy the whole thing and create a new unique name for it and lower then the price )
    "buy": "100" (the price you have to pay to buy it from the npc, here it would be 100)
    "img": "http (just get the item from Items , then click on the picture, then rightclick "open in new tab" and you have your url)
    "item": "hemp seed" (just take the wiki name in lower case)
    "sell": "90" (the price the npc will pay you for the item - here 90)

    I hope I was able to help, just tell me if you need any further help
