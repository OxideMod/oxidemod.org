    Per the forum rules, we do not allow forks or re-submissions of someone else's code for both licensing purposes (you didn't write it, you don't hold any rights to it) and out of respect for the original author and contributors. If you write a plugin that is entirely your own, you are welcome to submit it for review, though contributing to existing plugins is encouraged, but in cases like this where the author isn't around much that can be a bit challenging. If someone wants to share their own version privately, that's fine, but they aren't allowed publicly on the forums for moderation purposes as well as they may have changes that the plugin's author or maintainer doesn't agree with.

    In this case, the change is a feature, not a fix, so it's up to the author or maintainer to change it how they see fit. @Mughisi and I will generally provide fixes for plugins if the author isn't available, but new features are generally up to the person developing the plugin. With that said, this plugin is somewhat considered unmaintained now as @Nogrod hasn't responded recently, so it may be passed to someone else to maintain soon.

    So with that being said, if you have any further concerns unrelated to the usage of this plugin, feel free to request a PM and I'll be more than welcome to continue the discussion, otherwise let's keep this thread's posting to issues or requests related to the released version.
