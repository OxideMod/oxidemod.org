  1. Jake Rich

    Jake Rich submitted a new resource:

    Weapons On Back - Renders the player's best weapon on their back, and best sidearm on their hip

    View attachment 29246 View attachment 29247

    Renders the player's best weapon on their back, and best sidearm on their hip. Can be configured to check the player's entire inventory (default), or to only check the player's hotbar.

    Use the command "/weaponsonback displaymode 0" to only check the hotbar, or "/weaponsonback displaymode 1" to change it back to check the player's inventory.

    Please report and issues you find. Thanks!

    [KNOWN ISSUES]
    -Doesn't render bow yet
