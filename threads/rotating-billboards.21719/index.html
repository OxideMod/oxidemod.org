    k1lly0u submitted a new resource:

    Rotating Billboards - Create signs that rotate on the spot

    A simple plugin that allows you to create signs that rotate on the spot.



    Chat Commands
    /rot add - Adds a rotator to the sign you are looking at
    /rot remove - Removes the rotator from the sign you are looking at
    /rot remove all - Removes all rotators and wipes data
    /rot start - Starts rotating the sign you are looking at (if registered)
    /rot stop - Stops rotation of the sign you are looking at
