    Survey Info - Displays results from Survey Charges in chat with a % score.

    Survey Info allows players with permission to view the Survey Results in chat, also works with Gather Manager for increased rates.

    Permissions

    This plugin uses Oxide's permission system. To assign a permission, use grant user <name or steam id> <permission>. To remove a permission, use revoke user <name or steam id> <permission>.
