    [DOUBLEPOST=1516555416][/DOUBLEPOST]I have a couple suggestions.

    One issue players will see, the same issue with Running Man, is that the players may not want others to know where they are located. With this mod that's unavoidable so given that penalty, players must get something really good in return and not just some penalty for holding an item that doesn't give them any valuable advantage.

    Here's what I would suggest:

    1. Player holding the AK in their belt, will gain whatever items you specify at whatever interval you specify. For instance, if I have the AK, I will get one satchel charge every 5 minutes. If you want to go further, make it so that there is item graduation. For instance, if I have it for 5 minutes I get a satchel charge. If I have it for 15 minutes I might get C4, etc. etc. Time doesn't reset unless another player put it in their belt, but it stops when the player removes it from theirs.
    2. Make timed messages to alert the player of the rewards they get
    3. Make timed messages to alert the other players that they received an award
    4. Make messages (if not exist) to alert when the AK changes hands
    5. Make messages (if not exist) to alert when the item is dropped
    6. Make message when it's put into the belt to alert player they will start getting rewards
    7. Allow the AK to have it's skin set to any skin
    8. Make it work with LustyMap
    With all this players would really want the AK. Now I think players won't see a real need to have it and others won't see a need to seek it out.
