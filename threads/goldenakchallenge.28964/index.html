  1. Lamego

    Lamego submitted a new resource:

    GoldenAKChallenge - Deploys a special AK, that is tracked in the map for capture

    This plugin deploys a special (golden) AK that is continusly marked in the map with a red dot, the goal of the challenge is to capture and keep the AK.

    Features:
    - Tracking events: player pick & move, item drop, container placement
    - Prevents: AK can not be moved to recyclers, research tables or loot containers
