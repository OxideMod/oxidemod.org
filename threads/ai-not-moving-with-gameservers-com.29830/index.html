  4. [UHL]MULE

    When you open the Gameservers console there are 4 buttons on the right side of the screen. The second one one is the config. Click on that, change the last line to +nav_disable false -nologs -load . Click submit. Your server will do a restart and the animals should be moving.
    [DOUBLEPOST=1520714869][/DOUBLEPOST]To open the FTP files you have to down load Waterfox browser and install FireFTP with it. Open the console, click on Info, then click the line next to FTP URL: , that opens the Mods menu. You'll need your server password to access the FTP files.
     
    [UHL]MULE, Mar 10, 2018
