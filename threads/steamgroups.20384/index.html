  1. Wulf Community Admin

    Wulf submitted a new resource:

    SteamGroup - Automatically adds Steam group members to specific permissions group

    SteamGroup automatically adds Steam group members to specific permissions group, checking for updates every 60 seconds. Users no longer in the Steam group will automatically be removed from the permissions group as well.

    Configuration
    You can configure the settings in the SteamGroup.json file under the oxide/config directory.
    Code:
    {
  "PermissionsGroup": "Steam",
  "SteamGroup": "OxideMod"
}
