    carny666 submitted a new resource:

    SimpleMapKeeper - Automatically saves and restores players equipped maps

    Automatically saves and restores players equipped maps.

    You don't really need to use these command as the map is saved automatically when it's placed in your belt and restored when you re-spawn.

    /simplemap save - Manually save reference to equipped map.
    /simplemap restore - Manually restore reference for equipped map.
    Click to expand...
    Known 'bugs':
    • if you die while holding the map, sometimes your map is lost.
    • If you die, then quit before re-spawning your map is lost.
