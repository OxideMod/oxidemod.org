  1. Jake Rich

    Jake Rich submitted a new resource:

    UpgradeTimer - Twig has a configurable cooldown before it can be upgraded after being placed.

    This plugin prevents players from placing and upgrading twig instantly, which can be abused during online raids to seal up an entrance or replace a wall in less than a second after it is destroyed.

    Config: Configuration file is UpgradeTimer.cfg.

    Change "UpgradeDelay" to specify the number of seconds a player must wait before twig is upgradeable.

    Default value is 10 seconds.

  5. Jake Rich

    FunkyRusty said: ↑
    can you please give me one if any, an example why a player would want a cooldown to upgrade his 10 hp building structure?
    Click to expand...
    Lets say you are online raiding me.

    As you are blowing into my base, I hide in the corner and wall myself in with 2 layers of metal walls. You now can't get my loot unless you spend a ton of explosives just because I happened to be online.

    With this plugin activated, if I hide in the corner and try and upgrade my stone walls to metal and place down twig and upgrade to metal.

    As you blow in, you see a bunch of metal walls. As the walls keep their health when upgrading, the walls that were stone are 500/1000 health, and the twig walls that I placed and upgraded are still 10/1000 health. You know that those walls were just placed, and you are able to blow through them with a single explosive.

    It makes online raiding a HELL more viable, especially on servers with b-grade (where people just spam place walls at the place you blew in).
