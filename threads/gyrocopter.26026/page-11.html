  11. Thenitro219

    Hello. What is Oxide's permission system and where can I download it?
    Click to expand...
    You dont have to "download" the oxide permission system. You go into your console and type the permission you want to give someone or a group
    For example if you want to give a user permission to build a copter do oxide.grant user username gyrocopter.build
    or if you want to give it to everyone do oxide.grant group default gyrocopter.build
