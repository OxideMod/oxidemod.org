  5. mspeedie

    You can adjust the population on them like: bear.population, boar.population... etc.
    RUST Admin Commands & Server Commands List for 2018
    Now here is where you may get unhappy. If you want to increase the spawn rate it affect all things, not just animals:
    spawn.min_rate 0.5
    spawn.max_rate 1
    Not certain if these affect animals or just nodes:
    spawn.min_density 0.5
    spawn.max_density 1

    This might do it but I have not used it:
    spawn.fill_populations
    or look at fuji's paid mod (SPawn Control) to control populations, seems the feedback on it is positive.
     
    mspeedie, Apr 21, 2018
