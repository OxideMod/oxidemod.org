    -1 tells the plugin not to remove or add the item in question.

    100 wolves and 100 bears might seem like a lot but on a 4K map, they'll still be scarce at that setting. I run 100 bears and 250 wolves on my server and they are pretty easy to find, but not all over the place.

    The plugin turns it's first cycle after 15 minutes with the default config, you can force the cycle by doing /cspawn (or cornu.spawn in console/rcon). You can use /cdump (or cornu.dump) to confirm they are there.

    And yes, 100 = 100 bears on the map
