    StashControl - Manage small stashes

    StashControl allows to set decay system for small stashes or make small stashes completely undying.

    Configuration
    You can configure the settings in the StashControl.json file under the oxide/config directory.
    In ConstDecayStashes mode you can modify some settings:
    RepairOnLoot - repairs small stash to 100% health every time on player loot;
    HiddenDecayOnly - toggles unhidden small stashes to no decay.
