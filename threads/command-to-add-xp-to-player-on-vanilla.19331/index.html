  4. Colon Blow

    The only thing you can do in Vanilla is admin only (as of now. might change)
    Log in game as admin, open F1 console. This will give yourself xp or levels.

    xp.add 1000
    xp.setlevel 50
    xp.reset

    that's about it I think. But as everyone said. you cannot give it to other players without a plugin.
