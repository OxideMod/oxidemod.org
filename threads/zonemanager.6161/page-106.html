  18. LUNARKINGDOM

    I am trying to get Privatezones to work with Zone Manager but it does not recognize the people I add to my group here is how I am trying to achieve it have I missed any steps or anything?

    First I load both plugins "Zone Manager" and "Privatezones" into the oxide plugin folder then do the following chat commands:

    /zone_reset = clears all zones

    /zone_add = stand in center of zone and type this make sure you are at ground level

    /zone name "SAFEZONE"

    /zone radius 500

    /zone autolights true

    /zone enter_message "YOU HAVE ENTERED THE SAFE ZONE"

    /zone leave_message "YOU HAVE LEFT THE SAFE ZONE"

    /zone pvpgod true

    /zone pvegod true

    /zone sleepgod true

    /zone undestr true

    /zone noplayerloot true


    Now that my zone is setup I type:


    /zone_list


    The zoneid is the number on the left of my zone name "SAFEZONE", next I take this number and place it in the "zoneid" and the permission you want in "permission" associated with that zone, example /pz add <zoneid> <permission> or in our case replace the xxxxxxxx with the number you got on the previouos step:

    /pz add xxxxxxxx zones

    "zones" being the specific zone kicker tag.


    Next I added the kick players chat command:


    /zone eject true


    Next I added an sob group with oxide permission system:


    group add sob


    Then I added the sob group to the zones permission I created previously:


    grant group sob zones


    Next I added sob members to the sob group:


    usergroup add username sob




    My result was everything seemed setup correctly but the players even after I added them to the group get rubber banded out of the zone. What did I miss? I was pretty thorough going over the individual instructions for the oxide permission system, Zone Manager and Privatezones.
