  17. GoDANKorGoHOME

    Go into your current zones file and remove all nodecay flag references. If you put nodecay on a zone it will spam this until you restart and either remove the zones or remove the nodecay manually from the ZoneManager/data file. You can probably also just remove any nodecay flags via editing the zone in-game if you don't like to mess with files, but it will still require a restart to get the NRE's to stop.
