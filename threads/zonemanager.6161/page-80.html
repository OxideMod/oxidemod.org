  6. Steenamaroo

    That's how you do it.
    What happens when you do it?

    /zone_add creates a sphere zone with default radius
    /zone size "10 10 10" (for example) turns it into a cube of 10m.
    /zone size "10 1 10" would make a 10m 'pizza box'

    The size parameter is to edit an existing zone - not to create one.
