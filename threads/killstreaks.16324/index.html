    KillStreaks - Keep track of player kill streaks and either reward or punish them

    Keep track of player kill streaks, broadcast custom killstreak messages and have the option to reward them with supply drops or punish them using other means. With UI notifications and a leaderboard

    Streaks currently available - I am looking for more ideas, feel free to leave any suggestions in the support thread
    Code:
    0 - None
