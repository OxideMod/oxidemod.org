  4. Smeag

    @q1LLa - Just go ingame with admin account, walk to the place where you want to add a fall site, and do /addfallsite 200 The Valley.
    200 here is the radius of the area where it could possibly fall. Maybe 200 is too much for the valley town: sometimes it falls over buildings of players around the town.
    Try 100, or 80, like that:

    ~ walk to the middle of The Valley and type:
    /addfallsite 100 The Valley

    Then you can remove the old registered place with the same name with /removefallsite ID ~ where ID is the index showing in front of the place that you can see listed with /listfallsites
     
    Smeag, Jun 6, 2016
