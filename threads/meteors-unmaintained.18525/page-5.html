    Click to expand...
    The map is approximately 5500 or so meters. What I did is I set the regional locations that came default with the plugin at 2500 and the rad town/more specific locations to 1000. That gave me a good mixture between meteors that fall randomly around a whole region and meteors that fall within 1000 meters of a hot spot. If you want completely random I'd suggest setting the distance for all to 2500+. You could do like... 4000 but then half the meteors will fall outside of areas that players can reach.

    Easiest way to measure meters is to use the beacon and measure you distance from one point to one of your stakes.... to give you a better idea of the distances.

  19. CaptainNebulous

    [SBS] MaximusSavage said: ↑
    throwing a meteors does not drop stuff no its only for fun use really only the set location on map will drop
    Click to expand...
    /throwmeteor locationID will throw the real ones at one of the pre-determined locations. If you open the location data file, the ID's are determined by what order the locations are in. The first locations is location ID 0. So you count from 0 on up. However you are correct that simply /throwmeteor or /throwmeteor playername will not spawn a real meteor. Just /throwmeteor locationID.
