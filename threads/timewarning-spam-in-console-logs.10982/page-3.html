  9. Deicide666ra

    From what I understand this warning pops up when a server tick takes too long to return. I don't know what the default server tick time is, I'm guessing it's in the order of 30 ms or so usually. Lots of things can affect and hold the server, it could be DDOS, it could be a mod going wild, it could just be the mutualized server you are on running out of CPU or ram, it could be the HDD choking up loading something/saving something, etc.

    From my experience with Legacy, you want your mods to do as little writing and reading from and to disk as possible and not too often either. You might have a mod that's going crazy on memory usage (ex: it allocates something on a frequently called hook and fails to deallocate it), or even CPU usage (ex: the plugin stores information on all player actions and each tick it looks through that info to compile stats --- as time goes, that information stash is increasing in size and gets slower and slower to go through, eventually choking the server).

    Another case I've had was with the playrust.io pluggin a while back (this is a suspicion, I could never confirm it). This extension sends information to the site constantly to update player position, chat, etc. and if the domain becomes unresponsive, I assume it can hang the server. On that night where I had insane cycle times, when I tried going on playrust.io half the time it would just hang there for 30-60 seconds before telling me the site was unavailable. Could be pure coincidence too, it was an update night and things can get hectic on those days.

    I would look with your host to see if they can monitor the memory and CPU usage of your rust server process. I would also look at your mod configs/logs to see if there is anything suspicious or huge hidden in there. You might want to remove mods (and extensions) one by one and see if you find the culprit. Also inquire with your host about the possibility of it being a DDOS attack, they should be able to identify that.
