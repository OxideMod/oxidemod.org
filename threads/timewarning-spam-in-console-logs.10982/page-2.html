  19. Meridian

    You guys could also try deleting all of the animals you have on your server (or the really high populated ones) if the AIHandler message persists.

    If you have full access to your server directory, in console type 'dump' then go to your ~/server/diagnostics folder. There will be a numerically named subfolder for each time you perform a dump. Inside the GameObject.Count.txt file you can see an actual integer count of each game object spawned on your server.

    Example: del autospawn/animals/bear

    That would delete all of the bears. If you have the spawn.max_rate and spawn.max_density variables adjusted too high, your server can become overpopulated quickly and that can cause some serious lag. I once set it way higher than I meant to and didn't pay attention for a few hours, came back, and bears had literally killed every other animal on the map and were on the verge of crashing the server.

    After deleting them, turn your spawn rate down a bit, and they'll slowly start spawning back into your map.

    The Net.sv probably won't go away though with this unless you go crazy with the del command and remove forests, etc, and you still may get them if the root cause is with a mod and not the server being bogged down with entities.
