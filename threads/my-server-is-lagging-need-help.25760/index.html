    You have quite the opposite problem of the other individual, if it is not an issue from the provider or the dedicated server itself, then it may be your rust server.

    -For starters, take one plugin out at a time and track your dedicated server's performance (CPU, Memory, etc).
    -Re-trace your steps to see if you added anything new that might have been the cause of decreased performance.
    Other options: Check for DDos attacks (contact your provider), players themselves could perhaps be the cause of the lag (rock spamming, or w.e rust allows players to do these days which will make the sever heavily lag).
