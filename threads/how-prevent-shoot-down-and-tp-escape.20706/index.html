    You can show the permissions of a group/user with this:
    Code:
    show group/user [Name or SteamID/group name]
    Grant permission with this:
    Code:
    grant [group/user] [groupname/username or SteamID] permission
    Replace "grant" with "revoke" to remove the permission.
