  4. ignignokt84

    You need to add some null checks to your code. It's better to have a few "extra" null checks than too few. I would check if HitInfo, HitInfo.HitEntity, and HitInfo.damageTypes are all non-null before doing anything else. But from the context I suspect HitInfo.HitEntity is your culprit, since c4 does damage to all entities within in its blast radius as opposed to most other weapons which are focused on one entity.
