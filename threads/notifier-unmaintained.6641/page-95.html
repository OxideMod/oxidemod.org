    Notifier | Minor Fix



    Read the rest of this update entry...

    Version 3.0 In Coming:
    The latest version is now the last Python version of Notifier, version 3.0 is now my top priority and will be the one I'll be working on for now on. This means I will be finally porting Notifier to C#, which will greatly improve it's performance even though that is not quite an issue with the current version, but will be faster and I will be trying to simplify things as they are now. Though, this will be a bit of a slow process as I am developing 3.0 as I learn C#, and also I will do my best to have Notifier running with all the features from the current Python version on day one. I do not have any estimated time on this, for the reasons I mentioned before, just know I have been working on it for a while and that now I am more focused on it.
    Even though my goal here is to just port Notifier to C#, I will of course port my other plugins to C# and maybe even work on new plugins in the future, but better than this I want to finally work on UI features, mostly for Notifier and Rank-ME.

    Now, who the fuck is SAM?
    A lot of you asked and wondered why I have added these random messages to Notifier about 'SAM', and here's why.
    SAM is actually a plugin I have worked before a few years ago for another game, which at the time a lot of its users thought I named it after me, but in fact I did not, 'SAM' is short Source Admin Menu, I just thought it cooler to name it SAM, and back in the days I did the same, I have added these random messages to SAM as sort of an inside joke.
    Now the reason I brought it into Notifier is just because a had some people from that time who recognized and asked me to bring SAM to Rust using Oxide, and I do intend to do so, but there is a lot to plan before working on it.

    And that's about it.

    See you guys soon.
