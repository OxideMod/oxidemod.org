    Why? Well because one does work properly, or not as it we want it to work.
    The simple reason is, the server may even have the default text size, but the text size can be big on low resolutions. I even tried to work around the issue but with no success.

    Code:
    :red:This is a:/end: :green:colored message.:/end:
    Output - This is a colored message.
