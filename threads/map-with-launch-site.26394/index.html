  5. UnicornWeed

    You need to be using a Map Seed (server.seed) which includes the Launch Site. As it's a huge monument I would suggest aiming for a Map Size (server.worldsize) of at least 3000. Any worldsize below 3-4k will have low possibility of being able to generate the Launch Site successfully.
    Either test out your own bunch of Map Seeds until you find one which includes the Site, alternatively go here --> Rust Live Map & Map Generator OR Rust Live Map & Map Generator and use one of the seeds already in use by other servers.
    Note: Changing your server.seed or server.worldsize will wipe all player buildings/ inventories.
