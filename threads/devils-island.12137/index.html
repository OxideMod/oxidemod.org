    Nick Holmes submitted a new resource:

    Devil's Island - Devil's Island Game Mode plug-in

    Summary

    Devil's Island is a complete game mode plug-in, designed to provide a bit more end-game content, and some structure to player interactions.

    Players compete to be the Boss, who can place a tax on resource gathering. Being Boss is has its obvious advantages, but he also has a huge target painted on his back; everyone knows where he is (to within about 150 meters). Kill the Boss to become the Boss - it's that simple.
