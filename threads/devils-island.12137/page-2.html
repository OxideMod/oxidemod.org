  9. Nick Holmes

    [DK] IIIBARCODEIII said: ↑
    Could you add it so if a rebel decides to follow the Boss again?
    Click to expand...
    There is quite a bit more to come around rebels/outlaws, and but I don't want to spoil the surprises just yet. Let's just say, I think I'm about 10% done at the moment.

    There is an underlying concept in this game mode that your decisions have real consequences, that will not be easy to escape. Certainly there will never be a "/unrebel" command - you're going to need the Boss forgiveness (somehow), or your going to need to change the Boss. What the rebels need is better tools to evade the Boss, and better tools to work together to solve their problem.

    The beautiful irony is that when a rebel finally kills the boss, he will immediately become the new boss. The reason the Boss is called the Boss (and not, example "King"), comes from the lyrics of The Who's "Won't Get Fooled Again" - "Meet the new boss, same as the old boss".
     
    Nick Holmes, Oct 7, 2015
