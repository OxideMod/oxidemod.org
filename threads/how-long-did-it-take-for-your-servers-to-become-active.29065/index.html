  2. Quantum

    I used to host a server a long time ago. The way we got players was to find clan leaders. We had friends that owned huge clans and as they started joining people saw that the server was active and people just kept joining.

    Try to keep the following to a minimum:
    • Server lagg
    • Ping
    • Hackers
    • Admins that abuse
    It's also a good idea to not add plugins like info panel or anything that clutters the screen. I did some research before making a server and people don't like that. They like to keep it as basic as possible. Basically just stacksizecontroller, gathermanager and instant craft is what we had along with better chat and some other admin plugins. If possible when you see new players try to be friendly with them. Just don't be annoying. We had about 30 + players per day.
