    LaserHydra submitted a new resource:

    Timed Permissions - Allows you to grant permissions or groups for a specific time

    Timed Permissions allows you to grant permissions or groups for a specific time.

    The <time> argument should tell for how long the player should have the permission. Ex: /grantperm LaserHydra timedpermissions.use 30d gives LaserHydra the permission 'timedpermissions.use' for 30 days.
    • d = days
    • h = hours
    • m = minutes
