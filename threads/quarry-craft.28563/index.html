    Quarry Craft - Returns the ability to craft quarries back!

    So, this devblog(189) our beloved Facepunch removes teh ability to craft minig quarries at all. This plugin is here to get it back.

    Firstly I wanted to allow players research them, but there is a clientside problem - the client tells the player that he can't research it, so from the serverside it's impossible.

    We also can't add it to the crafting grid, as long as it's clientside...

    So there is a command(can be changed in the config):
    /quarry - start the crafting of the mining...
