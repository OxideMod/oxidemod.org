    StashSniffer - Keeps track of all small stashes and where they are placed

    Tracks small stashes and keeps the information stored. You can then get a count of total small stashes, or see where they are in a radius or over the entire map

    Chat Commands
    /stash near <radius> <opt:time> - Show all small stashes within the <radius> specified, <opt:time> being the amount of time they will be shown (default 10 seconds)
    /stash all <opt:time> - Show every small stash location on the map for <opt:time> (default 10 seconds)
