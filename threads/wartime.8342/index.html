    Fraccas submitted a new resource:

    WarTime - Allows admins to set a dedicated time for the use of siege weapons

    This plugin allows admins to use /wartime to toggle a time that players are allowed to use siege weapons on the server. You may add up to three admins to the cfg file at the moment, and you can let the mod kick or ban people who siege during peace times.

    Commands
    /wartime     - Toggles WarTime/PeaceTime. Admins Only.
    /checkWarTime - Tells the caller whether it's a time of Peace or War.

    CFG (WarTime.json)
