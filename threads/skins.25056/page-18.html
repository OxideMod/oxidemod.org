    Click to expand...
    @Taskmaster
    A shortname is a name for devs to reference different objects without having to type out its full actual name, and its very useful to organize different subsets of items (different types of rifles, pistols, shirts, boots, etc.)

    Ex.
    • Assault Rifle's shortname is "rifle.ak"
    • LR-300 Assault Rifle is "rifle.lr300"
    • Wood is "wood"
    • Hide pants is "attire.hide.pants"
