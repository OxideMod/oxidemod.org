  20. theeye

    puchuumetal said: ↑
    not sure if im missing something but i tried to add a skin from the workshop via console commands, seems like it took but when i open the /skin container and try to add item it just gets shoved back out


    apparently skin wasnt on approved list, will it get updated to include more items or is there a way to force it in?
    Click to expand...
    You have too many skins listed for that item. You will never get that skin because it is at the back/bottom of your list. And your list does not rotate your skins. When you do your skinslist. Try to keep the number at 29. You only have 30 spots to play with. And the 30th spot is where you drop "Your" item you want skinned. To cheat a bit. Open your .json folder and remove one of the skin codes at the top of your list. Taking the numbers out and keeping the dbl "". Then just type your new skin number in it. That way you have "placed" it in the list where you know you will get it. And it's not at the bottom. All those skin codes that total more than 29 will never be seen until others have been taken out to allow them to move up the list to being visable when you call on them.
