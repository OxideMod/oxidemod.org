Being forced to overpay for add-on content for an early access game that may or may not ever be finished, or could be bankrupt and not developed or supported at any moment is a pretty shitty business model, at least from the consumers perspective. While the game is in this state, I would clearly have preferred that the people making skins for it, were doing so freely, because they have the time, skill and passion to be part of the community that strives to make this game better together, like most of the great modders on this forum. And when the game actually is officially released, and we get to know what kind of game it actually is, THEN we could revisit the paid content workshop.

    I am not generally playing the game as it is meant, though...as I'm usually more of a creative builder. As such there are basically 3 plugins that has made me play this game past the 1000 hrs mark: copypaste, signartist and skins. Without the ability to continue on huge building projects through wipes, or without the ability create variety with a huge list of skins, I would have dropped this ages ago. In this mode the game actually has kind of a zen-like calm, as opposed to the PTSD paranoia inducing vanilla gamemode. I spent almost an entire day manually adding skins from the workshop to get this, and every time a skin gets accepted ingame, I sadly have to say goodbye to that skin in all my previous and future projects as it becomes a grey square in my skins plugin. Because paying for all of them, just isn't an option for me on a disability pension. And If I had money to throw away, I probably should upgrade my pc first, so I didn't have to play on anything less than the people who designs the skins, before I buy more of those. And to be honest: I would much rather donate any extra budget to the selfless modders here who donate so much of their time, knowledge and effort into making the game enjoyable for more than the saltiest PVP assholes who could have chosen about 9 billion other games to play to satisfy them.

    So for me, the whole skin voting-thing has become a double edged sword; I want to support and vote up the good stuff, but then I probably can't use it anymore if they get overpriced. Catch 22. I would like to see this plug be able to do more than one large crate though, and because the preview pics are almost completely useless; the workshop description as well. Would make it much easier to find and vote for them on the workshop as well. And find the right number in the long config file to remove when it gets greyed out.
