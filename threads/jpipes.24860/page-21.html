  8. RetardoBrain

    This is as much information as it is possible to obtain through the client.

    NO error messages of any kind at any point.
    section by section upgrade ONLY afeects HP of the pipe section. If the pipe has more than one section (over 2 meters), you ahve to upgrade all sections of the pipe individually.
    The pipes will ONLY accept changes to ON/OFF, Autostart, direction and and STACK (single/multi)
    ITEM FILTER will NOT toggle to active, regardless of how you upgrade your pipes.

    We just tried a full server shutdown (not the physical machine, only took the server offline for a restart), the pipes will revert to TWIG, regardless of what kind of upgrade you implemented on it prior to the restart.

    If you ask me, the latest update to Oxide, messed it up for Jpipes, in such a manner that they are setup and stays in TWIG, disregarding your grades to them. For some reason, I suspect the seerver is considering them to be walls, but also allows standard flow (1/sec)...
