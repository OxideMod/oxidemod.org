  11. JoeSheep

    Chief Slapaho said: ↑
    Is there a way for players to disable seeing the Announcements?
    Click to expand...
    Permission:
    GUIAnnouncements.toggle
    This will give access to the commands to toggle only your own exclusion from announcements.

    Command:
    /announcementstoggle [playername/64bit SteamID]
    announce.toggle <playername/64bit SteamID>
    This will toggle your's or the specified player's exclusion from announcements.
     
    JoeSheep, Jun 18, 2017
