    Is your brother attempting to connect to the server via your external IP address or your private IP address assigned to your computer via your router?

    I assume your server is running on Windows, so on the computer which is running the server go to start and search cmd.exe and run it. In the Command Prompt type: ipconfig and look for something which looks like this:
    IPv4 Address . . . . . . . . . . : 192.168.0.254 - YOURS WILL MOST LIKELY VARY FROM THIS

    Once you find this IP address get your brother to press F1 and type this in the console:
    connect 192.168.0.254 <-- replace this with the IP address which is shown in your ipconfig.

    This should theoretically work
