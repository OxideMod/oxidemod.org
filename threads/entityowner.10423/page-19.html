    There are players complaining that they are 100% privilege to build anywhere on the map. I had a complaint prior to this to ban them, went to them, and they explained that they didn't realize it was some glitch in Rust currently. 6/4/2016 I deleted them with the Builder plugin, using /erase. I was informed by FacePunch Studios to let some of the plugin designers that affect building privileges know of this issue, hopefully someone has this problem as well, or the developers can help, thank you!
