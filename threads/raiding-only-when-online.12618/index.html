    I can build my base for hours setting up traps, defence towers, rooms dedicated to fighting etc.
    Everything just so i can defend my base in different kind of ways during raids...
    But then in the end just to be raided while offline, and i bet this has happen to some of you guys.

    So my idea for a plugin which i really want and i will probly set up my own server if this plugin gets developed.
    Some kind of plugin that will force people to raid when the players are online.

    Option 1:
    As with some plugins you can use "/sethome" you could also use something like "/setclanbase" or something,
    then it checks if anyone in your clan/group is online anyone can raid you. But if all the clan members go offline all the foundations and bulding parts connected to the foundation you did "/setclanbase" on wont be able to take damage.

    Option 2:
    Pretty much the same as "option 1" but instead it goes for the Tool Cupboards.
    When everyone authorized to that tool cupboard is offline the building wont take damage.

    Cooldown.
    Ofcourse the plugin should have a cooldown, for example when the last player of the clan loggout, the building will still be able to take damage for 10-30min.
    (to prevent people abusing it by logging off during raids to instantly stop the raid, or maybe even place doors/walls and trap the raiders).

    Vulnerable.
    Also that the Plugin could tell for how long a clan/player has been offline. So if the clan/player has been offline for 2-3 days the building will be vulnerable again.
    (to prevent the map beeing filled with unbreakable bases).

    I know some people think that offline raiding is part of the game, especially for "Lone Wolfes"
    and i totally agree. But me and my clan is starting to get abit annoyed to always be raided offline as none wants to battle us =(

    So this would appeal to servers with alot of clans etc.


    Things i think the admin(s) should be able to change:
    - Cooldown time.
    - Amount of "/setclanbase" for example if you can only have 1 "unraidable base" while offline.
    - If the base should be invurnable or just reduced damaged and also the reduced damaged percent.
    - When the building should be able to take damage again after the clan/player has been offline for to long.


    so what do you think? anyone wants to try to make this? or anyone know someone i should contact?
    also what do you guys think? what could be added/removed?

    Click to expand...
    That is an incredibly astute point.

    The only feasible option is not to prevent offline raiding but to make it more expensive. There will always be edge-cases where players will take advantage of such a system. However, that can be a calculated risk. So, instead of canceling out damage completely, we can simply reduce it by a configurable percentage. That percentage can vary depending on the cooldown time.

    For example:

    Player logged out for 1 hour - 80% damage reduction
    Player logged out for 12 hours - 50% damage reduction
    Player logged out for 24 hours - 25% damage reduction
    Player logged out for 48 hours - 0% damage reduction

    Now the only obstacle is explaining to people why they are doing less damage to certain buildings and not others. The same logic could be applied to turrets.
