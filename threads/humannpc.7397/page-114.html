    Hey could you tell me. How to put clothes on them I can't seem to get the kids to work
    Click to expand...
    Hi yes its easy once you get the hang of it...

    First: make sure you have the ID of the NPC you want to dress and arm.

    Second: Make a kit for the npc to use by typing this into your chat
    /kit add "nameofkit"
    /kit items description "This is the kit" authlevel 2
    /kit hide true

    Third: Now edit your npc by typing: /npc_edit PLACEIDOFNPCHERE thenpress enter, you are now editing this npc

    Fourth: now type, /npc kit "NAMEOFKIT" you just made and you will see the npc is now wearing the items you selected.

    Fifth: If youre finished with this NPC you can now type: /npc_end to exit the editing mode

    Hope this helps..
