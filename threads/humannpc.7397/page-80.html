  10. Stefan Red

    Adanway said: ↑
    How do I dress to the NPC?
    Click to expand...
    You need the kit plugin.
    Then dress how you want the NPC to be dressed
    Then type
    /kit add Name
    /kit hide true
    /kit npconly true
    /kit items

    Then look at the NPC
    /NPC_edit
    /NPC kit name

    Done.
    Have fun
