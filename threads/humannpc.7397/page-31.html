    Are you setting the spawn and respawn times for an NPC that is still alive, or one that is dead? If you're doing this to a living NPC, I don't know how to help you, but if you're doing these changes to a dead NPC, from my experience, they won't retain those changes until they respawn. For example, if I set the respawn time to a living NPC to '600', kill him, and change it to '1', he will not respawn in one second, he will respawn in 600 seconds, and then from that point on, respawn in '1' second. Also as a tip, to force-respawn all NPCs, i've found you can just npc_add a new one, and npc_remove that fresh NPC.
