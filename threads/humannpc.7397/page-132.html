    @JET2000
    So the quick way is to do this:
    1. /npc_add
    2. /npc name "NAME"
    3. /kit add "KIT-NAME"
    4. /kit description "KIT-DESCRIPTION" authlevel 2
    5. (Fill your inventory with the items you want the npc to have. Also put the clothes on and weapons in the hotbar)
    6. /kit items (this will copy the items from your inventory to the kit)
    7. /npc kit "KIT-NAME"
    Done. Authlevel argument will make it so that ONLY admins and NPC's can use the kit.
