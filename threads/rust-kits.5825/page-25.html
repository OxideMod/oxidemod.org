    /kit add "starter" "A few essentials" -max2 -cooldown3600

    adds a kit with a maximum of 2, but no cooldown. Similarly,

    /kit add "starter" "A few essentials" -cooldown3600 -max2

    adds a kit with a cooldown but no maximum.
