  16. Raider160

    This may or may not be helpful to others trying to put Kits together. Because was I ignorant of other methods, I was by using the Give plugin and runing the inv.giveplayer "itemname" "amount" RCON command in Rusty to give my authlevel 2 character each flippin item, and then create the kit from there.

    Well some of you may know this, but for people like me that did not know, there is a MUCH easier way to set up your inventory on your authlevel 2 character before you run the /kit add command in game. Just hit F1 and go to the items tab and you will notice that each item has a BP, a 1, and a 100 on it. If you click them it will give you the item in either a quantity of 1 or 100. The BP did not seem to work. Again your character needs to be set as owner/admin "authlevel 2"

    Hope this helps some of you.
