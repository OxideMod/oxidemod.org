  12. Raider160

    Autokits are kits that are placed on your character after waking from death, or being a new player on the server. The player does not need to type anything. In other words, he starts off with this gear.

    Setup: Gear up your character that has an authlevel of 2 with whatever you want on the Belt, in the "Main" (your inventory), and in "Wear". After the character is set the way you want it, type the following:

    /kit add "autokit" "This is the autokit"
