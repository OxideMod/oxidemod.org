  9. =SKL=_Marlon

    Théogène said: ↑
    How to create a VIP kit?
    Click to expand...
    I did so:
    /kit add (kitvip)
    /kit items permission vip

    *This causes only groups or players that have permission "vip" can get this kit!
    *If you want you can get only once use: /kit items permission vip max 1
    * If you want it can get once a week use: /kit items permission vip cooldown 604800

    Sorry my bad English, I am Brazilian.
