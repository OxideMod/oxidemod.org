  14. Pois0n

    For something like security, you can certainly do it. You need 3 pieces, a contract (object which defines safe players and security guard), a zone (object that defines the land area where only safe players are allowed), and finally to hook into either OnPlayerAttack or OnEntityTakeDamage to assign points when a security guard damages a player not on the safe list!
