  1. Jake Rich

    Jake Rich submitted a new resource:

    Craft Queue Saver - Saves your crafting queue on disconnect and on server shutdown

    Saves the player's crafting queue when they disconnect or the server shuts down, instead of dropping all the items or deleting them all.

    When a sleeper is killed, all the items they have in their queue will be dropped, so player's can't store items safely on their corpse.
