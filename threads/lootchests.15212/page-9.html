  20. Noviets

    CaptainNebulous said: ↑
    Also just need a confirmation here. Is the number of spawns "spawn per location" or "total spawns among all locations" ? It used to be "spawn per location". Would prefer it to be "total spawns among all locations"
    Click to expand...
    The number of spawns is how many chests spawn at each location you define in the list. If you want to have a certain amount across all of them, just reduce that number down to equal the amount that you desire across your spawns. Divide your desired chest count by your spawn locations to get the number of chests you wish to spawn at each, to total the amount you want.

    So for example; if you want a total of 10 chests and have 5 locations, just set the spawn amount to 2
