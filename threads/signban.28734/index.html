    Tori1157 submitted a new resource:

    Sign Ban - Prevents users from updating signs.

    This plugin prevents players from updating signs, this includes uploading images with SignArtist.


    Permissions
    • signban.banning - Without this permission you have no access to the commands.

    Commands
    • sign (Displays the info & help message.)
    • sign ban (Bans user from updating signs.)
    • sign unban...
