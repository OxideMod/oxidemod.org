  2. Sionn

    Refer to this: Using Oxide's permission system | Oxide

    And then:
    1. Create a group for your moderators
    2. Assign permissions to that moderator group for all your relevant plugins
    3. Add your moderators to the moderator group

    That should be enough

    There's already a group called "moderator" by default with Oxide, just add the permissions and users to it.

    /usergroup add moderator USERNAME
    /grant group moderator PERMISSION
