  5. cowtrix

    The thing is, it's not a matter of how much your server can run, it's a matter of how much the player connecting to your server can run. We implemented the structure complexity limit because it was possible to build structures that caused lower-spec computers to run at less than 10fps, or made them unable to connect entirely. We don't believe that a player's actions should be able to negatively impact the performance of another player's client. If you wish to remove these limits, feel free, but please keep in mind the reasons they were implemented.
