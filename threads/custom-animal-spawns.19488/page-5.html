  12. pookins

    Permission

    grant user your name customanimalspawns.admin


    you type /cas
    and then get confirmation it was activated
    then /cas add (0-6) to choose spawn (press fire to create) ,
    and then /cas to close it
    animals are
    /cas add 0 (gets you zombies)
    /cas add 1 bears
    /cas add 2 boar
    /cas add 3 chickens
    /cas add 4 horses
    /cas add 5 stag
    /cas add 6 wolves

    Some caution is of course needed when adding dangerous animals in an area near you, i always make a foundation with a set of stairs to stand on
    so that i am not eaten before i can move away. and remember if you forget to type /cas after creating needed amount and try to shoot any of the animals you will actually create another one every time you shoot
