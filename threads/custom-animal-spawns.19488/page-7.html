    Hey Pookins,

    I suppose you decided to spawn zombie, so try "zombie.population" on your Rcon (or Rusty) console, maybe you have it = 0, then change it with "zombie.population x" (x is the population per square km).

    On my server after a restart, the animals just don't spawn.
    To fix it, i just spawn an animal manually with the "spawn" command (for example "spawn chicken") in the game console, and just after I do a "oxide.reload CustomAnimalSpawns" on the Rcon (or Rusty) console and all the animals spawn correctly then
