    Btw, where is the banlist located whenever you ban someone?
    Click to expand...
    Perm Banlist is located in your root directory (where your launch your server) called banlist.txt
    (If you need to unban someone, use the SteamID in this list, and use /unban SteamID in game or unban SteamID in your server console).

    Temp Banlist is located under oxide/data/AdminTools called TempBans.json.
