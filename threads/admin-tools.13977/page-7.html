    I added it due to requests. But honestly @LaserHydra could do this very easily, and it was sloppy. Here's my reasons;

    The current portall that was implemented ported everyone, even those that did not want to attend the event(s).
    It would be much better to have it based on timeouts like his Teleportation /tpa commands and open events for people to teleport themselves too.
    Admins could /setevent or something and have multiple event locations which users can teleport to using /event name or something like his /home command. Saves the admins having to teleport people, plus puts them at the location they need to be.

    I was thinking about all the different changes I could make and honestly it's something that requires a lot of detail. All of which doesn't suit the overall direction of this plugin, which is for moderation.
