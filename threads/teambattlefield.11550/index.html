  1. BodyweightEnergy

    BodyweightEnergy submitted a new resource:

    TeamBattlefield - Two teams, adjustable friendly-fire, spawn kits, unique team wearable item

    Similar to the current gamestyles on Battlefield-type servers, but with teams:
    • Endless, not Event-based, no scores kept (for now)
    • Two teams only
    • Players assigned to teams to keep numbers balanced (team might change if player disconnected unintentionally)
    • Common spawn kit is configurable (config file auto-created with popular kit)
    • Team-specific wearable item is configurable (ex. blue shirt for team 1, red hoodie for team 2)
    • Friendly-fire damage is configurable...

  5. BodyweightEnergy

    KΛWEN said: ↑
    Hi, can you use 2 spawnfiles to separate the team spawns? For example, I have an sniper event 2vs2, separate 100m and when someone dead, he respawn on his team spawn, not randomly.

    Thanks!
    Click to expand...
    Yes, and that's actually the only correct way to do it.

    The spawnfile you want to use for Team 1 should be named "tbf_t1_spawns",
    and the one for Team 2 should be named "tbf_t2_spawns".

    Spawnfile names can be changed in the config file, as well.
    Since you're doing a sniper event, you can customize the default loadout in the config file.
     
    BodyweightEnergy, Sep 4, 2015
