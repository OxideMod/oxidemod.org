  9. Jabian

    I've further adjusted the plugin.

    Previously - When users would connect to the server, they would always teleport back to the pre-set spawn points for red/blue teams. If someone has built a base and slept there, the next time they log in, they'll spawn back to the red/blue team spawn points.

    Changed - When a user connects, they will connect where they left from. It's only when someone dies, or has to press the "Respawn" button, that they will spawn back at the red/blue base.

    I've also blocked out the section which removes a user from a team, when they disconnect from the server. This way allowing the player to stay on the same team after they die or disconnect. (This may cause some issues with the teams becoming unbalanced. so hopefully the fix you have to change teams will do the trick)

    Thanks for great plugin, working a treat.
