    Hey guys, I recently had an idea that I believe is possible just need someone to step up and take change with this plugin!


    Health bar:

    Health bar is a a system that works very much like a health indicator above your head. This would also work on animals too if possible. So why would people need this? Good question, many servers are functional with PVP, having a health bar will help their players navigate towards a better understanding of there target, and or a fellow friend who needs healing! This plugin would include a progress bar made by text above there head with many changing colors. A good symbol for the health could be ⬛ and or ❤ or ♡ along with a progress percentage next to it. Ex) [⬛⬛⬛⬛⬛] {0}% << I dont know if the symbols would work because when I used console or chat this came up: Gyazo - 049e5af9ade37dc5951d4be730a2e450.png .. So I am aware plugins are a little more custom but if that does not work you can still use percentage bars. Ex) (Health {0}%). Another feature this offers is custom coloring for each health state. So by default for example, your health would be green during 100-75, yellow 74-50, orange, 49-25, and red 24-0, all completely config-able to the server owners needs!

    That is all I can really think of for this, thanks for reading guys and happy holidays!

    P.S. If someone is willing enough to take it another step, this can also include coldness, dehydration, hotness, etc. too!
