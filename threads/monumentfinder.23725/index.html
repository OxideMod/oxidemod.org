    PsychoTea submitted a new resource:

    MonumentFinder - Allows easy teleportation to monuments

    MonumentFinder is a quick n' easy plugin for admins to allow you to teleport to different monuments all over the map.

    Commands:
    - /mon list - Lists all the monuments
    - /mon {monument name} - Teleports you to the monument

    List of monuments: (taken from a 16km^2 map, seed 12345)
    - Airfield
    - Harbor
    - Harbor 2
    - Lighthouse
    - Military Tunnel
    - Powerplant
    - Radtown Small 3
    - Satellite Dish
    - Sphere Tank
    - Trainyard
    - Warehouse
    - Water Treatment Plant
